Plants vs. Zombies™ 2 is a tower-defense strategy game where players defend their brain from waves of zombies by planting various plants on a grid-based lawn. Players collect sun by tapping it, a resource generated by Sunflowers or falling from the sky, then spend it to drag-and-drop plant icons from the UI onto 5 available lanes on the lawn. Each plant possesses unique offensive or defensive abilities, and Plant Food can be dragged onto a plant to activate a powerful, temporary special ability. Progression involves earning Seed Packets from completing over 300 levels across 11 themed worlds, which are used to level up plants, enhancing their stats and unlocking new abilities. Players can also compete in the Arena mode against others to achieve high scores on unique levels and climb leaderboards.
