Paper.io 2 is a real-time territory conquest game where players control a colored block, expanding their domain by drawing lines to enclose empty space or opponent-claimed areas. Players move their block by swiping or dragging on the screen, leaving a vulnerable trail that, if cut by an opponent or if the player runs into their own trail, results in elimination. The core objective is to claim the largest percentage of the map, strategically cutting off rivals' trails to eliminate them while defending one's own. Progression is driven by achieving higher scores and dominating individual matches, with new challenges and rivals constantly appearing to test player skill. The game features both online and offline play against other players or AI, with the ultimate win condition being to become the last player standing or to secure the most territory.
