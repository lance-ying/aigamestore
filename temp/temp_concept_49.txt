**SPACEPLAN**: Players begin by tapping a central "Generate Watt" button, earning 1 Watt per tap, which is the primary currency. These Watts are spent to purchase and upgrade various potato-based devices, such as "Potato Generators," which automatically produce Watts at a set rate (e.g., 5 Watts/second) even when the game is idle. Progression involves unlocking new devices and launching narrative-advancing "probes" by spending increasingly large sums of Watts, with each device upgrade typically boosting its output by 10-20%. The game scales difficulty by requiring exponentially more Watts for subsequent unlocks and probe launches across five distinct planets and two realities, encouraging optimization of idle generation. The core loop is to generate Watts, purchase upgrades, launch probes to advance the story, and ultimately uncover all narrative secrets without any traditional loss conditions.
