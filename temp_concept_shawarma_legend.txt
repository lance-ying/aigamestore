Shawarma Legend: The True Shawarma Experience!

Have you ever wondered what it’s like to run your very own shawarma restaurant? Immerse yourself in Shawarma Legend, the newest and most exciting cooking game that lets you step into a world of culinary delight. Fulfill customer orders with precision and keep your restaurant thriving.

Upgrade every aspect of your restaurant, from the freshest ingredients and most delicious shawarma recipes to the decor and advanced kitchen equipment. Compete against rival restaurants to become the best shawarma chef in town!

Game Features

- Available in your language: The game is fully available in English, both in voice and text.
- Funny Cartoon Animations: Enjoy animations that bring your shawarma restaurant to life.
- Exciting Gameplay with Endless Challenges: Endless fun with thrilling gameplay and never-ending challenges.
- Realistic Simulation: Experience the life of a shawarma chef in a game designed to be as close to a real restaurant as possible.
- Authentic Shawarma Preparation: Cut shawarma from the spit and wrap sandwiches realistically.
- Comprehensive Upgrades: Enhance your kitchen with top-notch equipment and decorations to speed up service and delight customers.
- Customizable Shawarmas: Let customers customize their shawarma sandwiches exactly how they like them.
- Diverse Customer Base: Serve an endless number of customers, each with their own shawarma preferences.

Can you rise to the top and become the ultimate shawarma legend? Your skills and dedication will be the key to success!
