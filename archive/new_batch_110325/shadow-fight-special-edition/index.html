Shadow Fight Special Edition

Battle through 7 provinces in epic 1v1 martial arts combat. Defeat increasingly challenging enemies and bosses using punches, kicks, and special combo moves. Earn coins to upgrade your weapons, armor, and abilities. Master the combat system to survive tougher opponents and ultimately defeat the final boss Titan. Each round depletes your opponent's health bar in a best-of-three format. Strategic upgrades and skillful execution are key to victory.

← →: Move backward/forward | ↑: Jump | ↓: Crouch | Z: Punch | SPACE: Kick | SHIFT: Special ability (when unlocked) | ENTER: Start game | ESC: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart to main menu | Combo Moves: → + Z (Forward Punch), → + SPACE (Forward Kick), ↑ + Z (Jump Punch), ↓ + SPACE (Crouch Kick)
