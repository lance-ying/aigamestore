Buddy Bash: Stress Relief Sandbox

Buddy Bash is a physics-based stress relief game where you interact with Buddy, a ragdoll character. Use various weapons and your bare hands to inflict damage, rack up points, and complete increasingly challenging levels. Each level has a target score you must reach before time runs out. Stretch, toss, punch, shoot, and explode your way through 5 unique environments. Earn Bucks to unlock new weapons and outfits for Buddy!

ENTER: Start game from main menu
ESC: Pause/Unpause during gameplay
R: Restart (return to main menu)
SPACE: Fire/activate currently selected weapon
SHIFT: Reset Buddy's position and health
Arrow Keys: Cycle through weapons (LEFT/RIGHT) and outfits (UP/DOWN)
Z: Quick select Hand/Punch mode
