Klondike Solitaire

Welcome to Klondike Solitaire! Your goal is to move all 52 cards to the four foundation piles, building them up by suit from Ace to King. Build tableau columns down by alternating colors (red on black, black on red). Flip face-down cards to reveal new moves. Progress through 5 challenging levels, each with unique difficulty settings and time constraints. Master the game by completing strategic moves, managing your score, and unlocking higher levels!

Arrow Left/Right: Navigate between piles (tableau columns, waste, stock, foundations) | Arrow Up/Down: Select cards within a pile | Space: Pick up/drop selected card, or draw from stock | Shift: Toggle between Draw 1 and Draw 3 modes | Z: Undo last move | W: Draw cards from stock to waste | A: Auto-complete (when all tableau cards are face-up) | S: Auto-move selected card to foundation | Enter: Start game from menu | Esc: Pause/unpause game | R: Restart and return to start screen
