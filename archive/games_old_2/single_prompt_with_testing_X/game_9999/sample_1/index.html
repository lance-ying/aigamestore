Mob Control

Guide your cannon-spawned mob through a gauntlet of gates and obstacles to destroy the enemy base! Blue gates multiply your units, red gates divide them. Time your volleys, thread through moving multipliers, and unleash Champion abilities to break through fortified defenses. Each level rewards efficient routing, smart ability timing, and minimal losses with score ranks from C to S. Chain blue gates perfectly for bonus points and avoid red gates to maximize your final army size!

Controls: Left/Right Arrow: Aim cannon | A/D: Fine-tune aim | Space: Fire units (hold to stream) | Q: Champion ability | E: Swap Champion | Shift: Slow-motion aim | Z: Restart level | Enter: Start/Continue | Esc: Pause
