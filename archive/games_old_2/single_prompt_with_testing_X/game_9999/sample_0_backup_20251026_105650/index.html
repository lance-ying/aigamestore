Mob Control

Command your cannon to spawn and amplify your mob army! Aim carefully and fire units through blue multiplier gates to grow your forces exponentially. Navigate through a dynamic battlefield filled with moving gates, speed pads, and obstacles. Use your Champion's special ability strategically to break through enemy defenses. Your goal: overwhelm the enemy base with your amplified mob while minimizing losses. Chain blue gates for massive bonuses, avoid red gates that reduce your forces, and achieve S-rank by maximizing survivors and speed!

Arrow Left/Right: Rotate cannon aim | Space: Fire units (hold for continuous stream) | Shift: Activate slow-motion aim assist | Z: Use Champion ability to clear obstacles | ESC: Pause/Unpause game | R: Restart current level | Enter: Start game / Continue to next level
