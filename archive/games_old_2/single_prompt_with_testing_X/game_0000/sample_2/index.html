GOLD MINER

You are a gold miner on a quest to collect treasure! Your claw swings continuously—press SPACE to drop it and grab gold nuggets, diamonds, rocks, and mystery bags. Heavier items take longer to retrieve. Each level has a 60-second timer and a money goal that increases with each stage. Meet the target to advance, or it's game over! Between levels, visit the shop to buy power-ups like dynamite and strength potions. Complete 10 unique levels and keep climbing through increasingly challenging layouts!

SPACE: Drop/release the claw to grab items | Z: Use dynamite (if purchased, destroys grabbed item) | Arrow Keys: Navigate shop menu (up/down to select, right to buy) | ENTER: Start game / Confirm shop purchase | ESC: Pause/unpause game | R: Return to start screen
