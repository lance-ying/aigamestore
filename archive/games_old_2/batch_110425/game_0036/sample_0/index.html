Innocent Revenger - Wall Maiden & Miden Tower

Command your party through the mysterious Miden Tower in this tactical RPG! Battle enemies using turn-based formation combat where positioning matters. Your unique hero, Mule the Wall Maiden, can merge with walls for defense. Defeat enemies to gain experience, level up to 999, unlock over 100 passive skills, and transmute items in your kiln. Complete floors to progress through the tower and face the ultimate boss!

Arrow Keys: Navigate menus, select party member/action/target | Space: Confirm selection in battle menu | Z: Cancel/go back in menus | Shift: Open transmutation menu (when available) | Enter: Start game from title screen | Esc: Pause/unpause game | R: Return to title screen
