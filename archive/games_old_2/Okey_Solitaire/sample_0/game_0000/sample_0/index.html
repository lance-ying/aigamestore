Okey Solitaire

Welcome to Okey Solitaire! Your goal is to arrange your 14 tiles into valid sets (3 or 4 tiles of the same number in different colors) and runs (3+ consecutive numbered tiles of the same color). Compete against 3 AI opponents to be the first to complete a winning hand and declare "Okey". Each turn, draw a tile from the center pile or an opponent's discard, then rearrange your hand and discard one tile. A special Okey tile acts as a wild card. Win rounds to progress through 3 increasingly challenging levels!

Arrow Left/Right: Navigate tiles in your hand or move a picked-up tile | Arrow Up: Focus on draw piles (center or opponent's discard) | Arrow Down: Return focus to your hand | Space: Draw from selected pile, pick up/place down a tile in hand | D: Discard the currently picked-up tile | Z: Declare Okey (attempt to win the round) | Enter: Start game from menu | Esc: Pause/unpause game | R: Restart and return to menu
