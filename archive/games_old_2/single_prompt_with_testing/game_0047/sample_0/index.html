どうぶつしょうぎ (Animal Shogi)

どうぶつしょうぎ (Animal Shogi) is a simplified chess-like game on a 3×4 board. Each player controls 4 animal pieces: Lion (king), Giraffe, Elephant, and Chick. Capture the opponent's Lion or move your Lion to the opponent's back row to win. Captured pieces join your hand and can be dropped back onto the board. The Chick promotes to Chicken when reaching the far row, gaining enhanced movement.

Arrow Keys: Navigate piece/square selection | SPACE: Select/Confirm piece or move | D: Toggle drop mode to place captured pieces | R: Restart game (from game over screen) | ENTER: Start game (from start screen) | ESC: Pause/Unpause game
