CREO ADVENTURE

You are a novice Creo trainer embarking on an adventure to capture and train powerful creatures called Creo. Navigate through diverse biomes, battle wild Creo and rival trainers, and build your ultimate team. Capture Creo by weakening them in battle and using capture items. Train your team, unlock new skills, and prove yourself as the ultimate Creo master by defeating all challengers!

Arrow Keys: Move character / Navigate menus | Z Key: Interact / Confirm | Space: Cancel/back in menus | Enter: Start game | Esc: Pause/unpause | R: Restart
