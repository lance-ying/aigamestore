Shadow Arena: Action RPG

Enter the arena as a legendary hero in this 2D action RPG! Battle through waves of enemies, defeat powerful bosses, and level up your character. Use strategic combinations of skills with different cooldowns and mana costs to overcome increasingly challenging foes. Collect gear drops to enhance your stats and unlock powerful skill upgrades. Your goal: survive all enemy waves and defeat the final boss to claim victory!

Arrow Keys: Move left/right and jump | Z: Basic attack (generates mana) | Space: Skill 1 - Power Strike (15 mana, 5s cooldown) | Shift: Skill 2 - Whirlwind (25 mana, 8s cooldown) | Enter: Start game | ESC: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart to main menu
