Kitty Powers' Matchmaker

Welcome to Kitty Powers' Matchmaker! As a matchmaking agent, your goal is to build a successful dating agency by pairing clients with compatible dates and guiding them through successful romantic encounters. Match personality traits and preferences, then help couples navigate mini-games during their dates to fill the Love Meter. Earn reputation points to unlock new venues and grow your agency. Achieve 75% Love Meter or higher to succeed!

Arrow Keys: Navigate menus and control mini-game actions | Space: Confirm selections | Z: Alternative action | Enter: Start game | ESC: Pause/unpause | R: Restart
