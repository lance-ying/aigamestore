Meridian 157: Chapter 1

You are David Zander investigating the mysterious Meridian 157 subarctic facility. Navigate through static scenes, collect items, and solve puzzles to uncover the facility's secrets. Use inventory items on hotspots, decipher codes, and manipulate mechanisms to progress. Each solved puzzle unlocks new areas. The mystery concludes when you solve the final puzzle revealing the truth about Meridian 157.

Arrow Keys: Navigate between scenes | Space: Interact with hotspots / Use selected inventory item | Z: Select/cycle through inventory items | Shift: Request hint (60-second cooldown) | Enter: Start game | Esc: Pause/unpause | R: Restart to main menu
