ROCKMAN X: Deep Log Protocol

Navigate through the Deep Log cyberworld as a legendary reploid warrior. Battle through waves of corrupted enemies across 3 challenging stages, collecting energy crystals and power-ups to enhance your abilities. Defeat each stage boss to progress deeper into the corrupted data. Survive enemy attacks while managing your health and energy to achieve victory.

Arrow Keys: Move left/right, aim weapon up/down | Space: Jump (press twice for double jump) | Z: Fire primary weapon (hold to charge for more damage!) | Shift: Dash forward (requires energy) | R: Restart game (from game over screen) | Enter: Start game | ESC: Pause/Unpause
