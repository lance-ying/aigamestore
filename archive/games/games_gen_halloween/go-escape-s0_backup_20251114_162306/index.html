Go Escape!

Guide a rolling ball through challenging platforming levels! Your ball automatically rolls forward, and you must time your jumps perfectly to clear gaps, avoid obstacles, and reach the goal. Watch out for vanishing platforms that disappear after you land on them, and moving blocks that can knock you off course. One mistake means starting the level over. Complete all levels to win!

SPACE: Jump (fixed height and distance) | ENTER: Start game from menu | ESC: Pause/Unpause game | R: Restart to main menu from game over screen
