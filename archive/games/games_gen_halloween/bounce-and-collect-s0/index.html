Bounce & Collect

Drop balls strategically into a physics-based arena filled with pegs, multipliers, and obstacles. Your goal is to maximize your score by positioning each ball drop to hit high-value multiplier gates (x2, x5, +10 balls) before landing in collection bins. Complete each level by reaching the target score with limited ball drops. Progress through increasingly complex levels with moving obstacles and unlock new ball skins.

Controls: LEFT ARROW: Move drop position left | RIGHT ARROW: Move drop position right | SPACE: Drop a ball | ENTER: Start game | ESC: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart
