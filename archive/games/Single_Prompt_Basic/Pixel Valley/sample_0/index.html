Pixel Valley

Pixel Valley is a simplified farming simulation where you manage a small plot of land. Plant and harvest crops, manage your energy, and earn money to expand your farm. The game cycles through days, and each crop takes time to grow. Your goal is to reach 500 gold by efficiently managing your farm and resources.

Arrow Keys: Move your character around the farm
Z: Interact (plant seeds, harvest crops, sleep)
S: Sleep when near bed (manual sleep)
SPACE: Switch between tools (hoe, seeds, watering can)
ENTER: Start the game
ESC: Pause the game
R: Restart the game

Energy Management: When your energy reaches 0, you become exhausted and will automatically sleep after 3 seconds if you don't find a bed. Tiles are highlighted as you move for better visibility.
