Cut the Rope

Cut the Rope is a physics-based puzzle game where you must deliver candy to Om Nom, a cute green monster. Cut ropes strategically to swing the candy through the level, collect up to 3 gold stars for bonus points, and avoid hazards like spikes. Use air cushions and bubbles to manipulate the candy's trajectory. Each level presents a unique puzzle requiring careful timing and planning.

ENTER: Start the game | ESC: Pause/unpause | R: Restart to menu | Arrow Keys: Navigate menus | Space: Magic Finger (once per level) | Z: Restart level | Mouse Drag: Cut ropes | Mouse Click: Activate elements
