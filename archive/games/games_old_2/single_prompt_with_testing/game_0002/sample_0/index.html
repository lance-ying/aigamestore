WORMS BATTLE

Battle against enemy worms in turn-based artillery combat on destructible terrain. Command your team of worms, strategically position them, and launch devastating weapons to eliminate all opposing worms. Each worm has 100 HP - reduce enemy health to zero while protecting your team. Master trajectory and power to land precision strikes that reshape the battlefield. Complete missions to unlock new weapons and prove your tactical prowess!

Arrow Keys: Move selected worm left/right during movement phase | Space: Jump (if on ground) during movement phase | Z: Fire selected weapon during attack phase | Shift: Cycle through available weapons | Enter: Start game from menu | Esc: Pause/unpause game | R: Restart and return to menu
