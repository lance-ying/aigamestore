RPG ヴィラン転生 (Villain Reincarnation)

You lead a party of villains seeking to conquer the realm! Navigate the overworld, battle enemies using turn-based combat, and exploit their weaknesses to unleash devastating Break Attacks. Earn Infamy (XP) through villainous acts to level up your party, unlock new skills, and upgrade your characters' classes. Explore dungeons to gather materials for expanding your evil base with facilities that enhance your power. Progress through the story to ultimate victory!

Arrow Keys: Navigate menus, move party in overworld | Space: Confirm selection, interact, advance dialogue | Z: Cancel/Back in menus | Shift: Toggle sprint mode in overworld (faster movement) | Enter: Start game | Esc: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart to main menu
