Destins - Le Jeu De La Vie 2

Navigate life's journey in this digital board game! Spin the wheel to move along a winding path filled with life-changing decisions. Make choices about education, career, relationships, and property that affect your Knowledge, Wealth, and Happiness scores. Land on event spaces to trigger special opportunities and challenges. Reach the Retirement space to tally your final score—the highest combined total wins! Unlock cosmetic rewards by achieving milestones along the way.

Arrow Keys: Navigate menus and make choices | Space: Spin the wheel | Z: Confirm selections | Enter: Start game | Escape: Pause/unpause | R: Restart from game over
