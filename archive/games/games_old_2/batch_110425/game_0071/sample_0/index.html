Blending Cafe Story

Welcome to Blending Cafe Story! Manage your dream cafe by creating delicious recipes, serving customers, and expanding your business. Combine ingredients to craft unique drinks and food items, then serve them to customers with different preferences. Earn money to unlock new ingredients, upgrade your cafe, and work towards achieving a prestigious 5-Star rating. Balance customer satisfaction, recipe creativity, and strategic upgrades to build the ultimate cafe empire!

Controls: Arrow Keys: Navigate menus | Space: Confirm/Create/Serve | Z: Cancel/Back | Shift: Switch View | Enter: Start | ESC: Pause | R: Restart
