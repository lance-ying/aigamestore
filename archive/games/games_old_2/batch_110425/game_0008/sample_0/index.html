Iron Marines - Tactical RTS

Command your elite squad through tactical real-time strategy combat! Deploy units, upgrade them on the battlefield, and use powerful hero abilities to defeat waves of enemies. Capture strategic points, build defensive turrets, and complete objectives to progress through challenging missions. Manage your energy wisely to deploy units, upgrade them, and activate devastating special abilities. Victory requires strategic positioning, smart unit composition, and timely use of hero powers!

Controls: Arrow Keys: Scroll map | Space: Deploy unit | Shift: Cycle unit types | Z: Hero ability | Enter: Start | ESC: Pause | R: Restart
