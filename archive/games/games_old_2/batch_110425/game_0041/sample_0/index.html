RPG Crystal Osa

RPG Crystal Osa is a turn-based RPG where you lead a party of heroes through dungeons filled with monsters. Battle enemies to gain experience, level up your characters, and learn new skills. Collect Ores from defeated enemies to craft powerful equipment. Your goal is to defeat the final boss on floor 5. If all party members fall in battle, it's game over.

Arrow Keys: Navigate menus, select battle actions, move in dungeons | Space: Confirm selection, advance dialogue | Shift: Cancel, go back in menus | Z: Open party menu (during dungeon exploration) | Enter: Start game | Esc: Pause/Unpause game | R: Restart (return to start screen)
