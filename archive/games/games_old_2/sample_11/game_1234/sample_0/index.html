Incredibox

Create amazing music by mixing beats, melodies, effects, and voices! Drag musical icons onto beatboxers to build your composition. Discover special sound combos to unlock new musical styles and earn bonus points. Progress through 5 unique levels, each with its own distinctive sound library. Keep experimenting to maintain your satisfaction meter and complete all combos to win!

ENTER: Start game | ESC: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart (return to start screen) | Arrow Keys: Navigate icons and beatboxers | Space: Assign focused icon to focused beatboxer, or toggle mute on focused beatboxer | Z: Clear all icons from beatboxers | Shift + Left/Right Arrow: Switch between unlocked styles
