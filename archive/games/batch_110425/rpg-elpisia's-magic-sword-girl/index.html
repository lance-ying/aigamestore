RPG Elpisia's Magic Sword Girl

Enter the world of Elpisia as Aldo, a warrior who fights alongside Eris, a magical sword maiden. Battle through dungeons in turn-based combat, managing HP, MP, and Action Points (AP) to unleash devastating weapon skills. Transform Eris into powerful magic swords that unlock unique abilities. Level up, enhance your weapons, and defeat increasingly challenging enemies to save the kingdom. Strategic use of skills and the Charge command to recover AP is key to victory!

Arrow Keys: Navigate menus and move in exploration mode | Space: Confirm selection / Interact | Z: Cancel / Back in menus | Enter: Start game | ESC: Pause / Unpause game | R: Restart (return to start screen)
