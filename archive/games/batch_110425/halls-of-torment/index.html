Halls of Torment

Battle through the Halls of Torment as a lone hero facing endless waves of monsters. Survive for 5 minutes while collecting experience orbs to level up and choose powerful abilities. Defeat enemies to gain gold and find rare items that enhance your stats. Face increasingly dangerous foes and mighty bosses that spawn at regular intervals. Your run ends if your health reaches zero, but each attempt makes you stronger.

Arrow Keys: Move your hero | Space: Activate special ability (when unlocked) | ESC: Pause/Unpause game | ENTER: Start game from title screen | R: Return to start screen after game over
