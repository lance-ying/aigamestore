Castle Alchemy: Alchemist's Magical Journey

Enter the depths of a mysterious underground castle as an alchemist. Manage your castle's resources, craft equipment using time-based alchemy, and assemble a team of adventurers to explore dangerous mazes. Navigate through interconnected nodes to uncover treasures and resources. Progress by delving deeper, unlocking new crafting recipes and powerful job classes. Balance resource management with strategic exploration to survive the depths.

Arrow Keys: Navigate maze nodes, select options in menus | Space: Confirm selection, advance time (hold to speed up idle crafting) | Z: Switch between Castle Management and Maze Exploration modes | Shift: Quick-craft when in crafting menu (uses materials instantly)
