Burger Chain Story

Run a burger restaurant empire! Combine ingredients to create signature burgers, serve customers to earn money, and expand your chain. Unlock new ingredients from suppliers, hire staff to boost efficiency, and compete against rival shops. Your goal is to maximize profits, customer satisfaction, and reputation while opening multiple branches to dominate the burger market.

Arrow Keys: Navigate menus | Space: Confirm/Serve | Z: Remove ingredient | Shift: Details | Enter: Start | ESC: Pause | R: Restart
