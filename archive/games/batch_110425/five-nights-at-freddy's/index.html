Five Nights at Freddy's

You are a night security guard at Freddy's Pizza Parlor. Survive from 12 AM to 6 AM for five consecutive nights while hostile animatronics hunt you. Monitor security cameras to track their movements, but be careful—every action drains your limited power supply. If power runs out or an animatronic reaches your office with the doors open, it's game over. Each night gets progressively harder with more aggressive enemies and faster power drain.

Arrow Keys: Navigate camera feeds and UI elements
Space: Toggle camera monitor view
Z: Toggle left door (hold to keep closed)
Shift: Toggle right door (hold to keep closed)
Left Arrow (when not in camera): Check left hallway light
Right Arrow (when not in camera): Check right hallway light
Enter: Start game (from start screen)
Esc: Pause/Unpause game
R: Restart to main menu
