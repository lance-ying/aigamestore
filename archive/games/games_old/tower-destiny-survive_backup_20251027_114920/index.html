Tower Destiny Survive

Welcome to Tower Destiny Survive! You control a fortified tower defending against relentless zombie waves. Move your tower horizontally to collect valuable blocks while shooting down incoming zombies. Use collected blocks to upgrade your tower's health and firepower between levels. Survive three increasingly challenging levels filled with basic zombies, speedy runners, and tough tanks. Can you survive the zombie onslaught and emerge victorious?

Arrow Left/Right: Move tower horizontally | Space: Fire weapons at zombies | Enter: Start game from main menu | Esc: Pause/Unpause game | R: Restart (return to main menu)
