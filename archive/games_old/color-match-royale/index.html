Color Match Royale

Welcome to Color Match Royale! Race against AI opponents to be the first to empty your hand of cards. Match the color or number of the top card on the discard pile. Use action cards strategically to skip opponents, reverse turn order, or force them to draw cards. Wild cards let you change the active color. Progress through 5 increasingly challenging levels, from friendly beginners to ruthless experts. Score points by winning rounds - the more cards left in opponents' hands, the higher your score!

Arrow Left/Right: Navigate through your hand to select a card | Space: Play the selected card (or draw if no valid play) | Z: Call "UNO" when you have one card left (Levels 4-5 only) | Enter: Start game from main menu | Esc: Pause/unpause during gameplay | R: Return to main menu from game over screen
