P5 Marksman

You are an elite sniper tasked with eliminating hostile targets across 5 challenging missions. Use your scope to zoom in, carefully aim at designated targets, and take them out with precision. Beware of civilians - hitting an innocent bystander results in immediate mission failure. Manage your ammunition wisely, reload when necessary, and complete each mission within the time limit. Earn bonus points for headshots and accuracy. Progress through increasingly difficult scenarios from training grounds to the final strike operation.

Arrow Keys: Pan camera view | W: Zoom in | S: Zoom out | Z: Quick scope toggle (2x) | Space: Fire weapon | Shift: Reload | Enter: Start game | Esc: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart
