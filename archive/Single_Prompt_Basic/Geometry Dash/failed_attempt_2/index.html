Geometry Dash

Navigate your geometric cube through a challenging auto-scrolling level filled with spikes, platforms, and obstacles. Time your jumps perfectly to avoid hazards and reach the finish line. The game speeds up as you progress, requiring increasingly precise timing. Complete the level to win!

SPACE: Jump (tap to hop over obstacles)
ENTER: Start game from title screen
ESC: Pause/Unpause game
R: Restart game (when game over or at any time)
