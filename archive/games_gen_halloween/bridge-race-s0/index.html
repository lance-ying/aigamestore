Bridge Race

Race against 3 AI opponents across multi-lane tracks to be the first to cross the finish line! Collect colored blocks scattered on the track and use them to build bridges over water gaps. Knock into opponents to make them drop blocks, which you can steal. Progress through increasingly challenging levels with longer tracks, wider gaps, and more aggressive AI.

Controls: Arrow Left/A: Steer left | Arrow Right/D: Steer right | ENTER: Start game | ESC: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart to main menu
