Dungeon Warfare

Defend your dungeon core from waves of invading adventurers by strategically placing traps along their path. Purchase and upgrade traps using gold earned from defeating enemies. Each trap has unique abilities - from dart shooters to spring launchers to lava flows. Prevent 20 adventurers from reaching your core to win. Watch your gold and plan carefully, as trap placement is permanent once confirmed!

Arrow Keys: Navigate trap selection menu and grid positions | Space: Confirm trap placement or upgrade selection | Z: Cancel current action or deselect trap | Shift: Speed up enemy wave spawning (hold) | Enter: Start game from START screen | ESC: Pause/Unpause game | R: Restart game (returns to START screen)
