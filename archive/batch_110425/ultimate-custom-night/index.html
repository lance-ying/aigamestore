Ultimate Custom Night

Survive a single night from 12 AM to 6 AM in your office while managing power and defending against multiple animatronic characters. Each animatronic has unique behaviors and attack patterns. Use doors, vents, and various systems to prevent jumpscares while monitoring your limited power supply. Collect Faz-Coins to purchase defensive items and survive until 6 AM to win!

Controls: Arrow Keys: Navigate menu selections | Space: Activate/toggle selected system | Z: Emergency power boost | Shift: Open Prize Counter | Enter: Start game | Esc: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart
