LIMBO

You are a boy lost in a dark, mysterious world. Navigate through dangerous environments filled with deadly traps, hostile creatures, and physics-based puzzles. Push crates, swing on ropes, activate levers, and avoid instant-death hazards like bear traps and giant spiders. Use precise timing and environmental interactions to survive. Your goal is to reach the end of this haunting journey through checkpoints that automatically save your progress.

Controls: Left Arrow: Move left | Right Arrow: Move right | Space: Jump | Z: Grab/interact with objects (hold to maintain grip) | Enter: Start game | ESC: Pause/unpause | R: Restart to title screen
