🍔 Burger Shop Story

Welcome to Burger Shop Story! Build and manage your hamburger restaurant empire. Create unique burger recipes by combining ingredients, hire and train staff to serve customers efficiently, and research new ingredients to expand your menu. Earn profits to upgrade your shop, unlock advanced recipes, and achieve the highest customer satisfaction rating. Your goal is to reach $10,000 in total revenue while maintaining a high satisfaction score.

Arrow Keys: Navigate menu selections and UI elements | Space: Confirm selection / Hire staff / Purchase items | Z: Cancel / Go back to previous menu | Enter: Start game | ESC: Pause / Unpause game | R: Restart (from game over screen)
