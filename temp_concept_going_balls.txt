What is Going Balls? 

Going Balls is an exhilarating, fast-paced ball game where your main goal is to control a rolling ball and guide it safely to the finish line on increasingly difficult tracks. It’s the perfect blend of a precision puzzle game and a heart-pounding speed challenge.
