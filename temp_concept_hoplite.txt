**Hoplite:** Hoplite is a turn-based tactical roguelike played on small, hexagonal grid maps, where players tap on adjacent tiles to move their Hoplite character or tap on an enemy within attack range to initiate combat. Each turn, the player performs one action (move, attack, or use an ability), after which all enemies on the map simultaneously perform their actions according to their AI patterns, such as moving 1-2 tiles towards the player or attacking. Progression in Quest Mode involves descending through procedurally generated dungeon levels, with players earning "Fervor" by defeating enemies, which can be spent at altars to acquire one of three randomly offered permanent ability upgrades (e.g., +1 spear range, +1 shield block, +1 jump distance). The game ends upon the player's health reaching zero, with the objective being to delve as deep as possible into the dungeon, while difficulty scales by increasing enemy density, variety, and health points every 5 levels. Missions, like "defeat 10 archers" or "reach depth 5 without taking damage," unlock new starting upgrades or character variants for subsequent runs.
