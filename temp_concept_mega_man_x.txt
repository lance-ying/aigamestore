The super masterpiece of the action game "MEGA MAN X” now powered up!
With variety of weapons and reinforcing parts, break Sigma’s dark ambition down!!

◆Power-up points
1.【Supporting Retina Display!】
“MEGA MAN X”’s old graphic was localized!

2.【2 Display Modes!!】
In addition to “REGULAR MODE” which displays whole screen of the game, “FULL MODE,” which displays full iPhone display, is available!

3.【Three Difficulty Levels!】
Story Mode comes with Easy, Normal, and Hard difficulty options.
Easy places extra platforms in stages, so you won't fall to your death, and confident players looking for a challenge will feel right at home on Hard!

4.【Ranking Mode! (Function supporting Game Center)】
Compete with other players in Ranking Mode!
Aim for the most points in Score Attack, rush to clear stages the fastest in Time Race, and see who can finish the most stages in Endless.
Polish your skills and aim for the top!

5.【80 Challenges!! (Function supporting Game Center)】
20 challenges in the Opening Stage of “STORY MODE” (*1)!!
There are 60 in following stages, so there are 80 challenges altogether!
※(*1) does not supports Game Center.

6.【Support Features to Help You Progress!】
Those who don't know where to find the upgrades in-game, or who just want to power up right away, can easily get upgrades through the Customizations screen!
There are a range of support options, like Full Armor and All Weapons, to help you progress through the game smoother!
Try discounted “Perfect Set” to be strongest!
You can also change the BGM to the arranged versions to give the game a different feel!

Note: Support features and arranged BGM are paid content.
