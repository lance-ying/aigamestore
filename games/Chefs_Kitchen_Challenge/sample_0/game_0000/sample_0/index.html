Chef's Kitchen Challenge

Welcome to Chef's Kitchen Challenge! Master the art of cooking through precise mini-games. Complete recipes by chopping ingredients, mixing batters, and cooking to perfection. Progress through 5 levels of culinary mastery, from simple salads to gourmet three-course meals. Execute each cooking step with precision and timing to achieve high scores and unlock new challenges!

ENTER: Start the game | ARROW KEYS: Navigate menus | SPACE: Confirm / Timed actions | ESC or SHIFT: Pause | R: Restart | Z: Go back in menus | MOUSE: Click and drag for chopping and mixing
