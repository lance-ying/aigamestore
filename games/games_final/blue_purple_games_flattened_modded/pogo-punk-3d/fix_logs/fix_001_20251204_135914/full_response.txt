<analysis>
- Issue identified: The user finds the gravity too strong (falling too fast/heavy) and the rotation sensitivity too high (spinning out of control easily).
- Files affected: `globals.js` contains the physics constants for gravity and rotation.
- Changes needed: 
    - Reduce `GRAVITY` from -0.015 to -0.009 to make the player floatier and falling less punishing.
    - Reduce `ROTATION_SPEED` from 0.05 to 0.012 to significantly lower the angular acceleration, making tilting more controllable.
    - Reduce `MAX_ROTATION_SPEED` from 0.15 to 0.1 to cap the maximum spin rate.
</analysis>
