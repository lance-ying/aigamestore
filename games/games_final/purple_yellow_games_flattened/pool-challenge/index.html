Pool Challenge

Welcome to Pool Challenge! Your objective is simple: use the white cue ball to pocket all 15 colored balls into the holes. Aim carefully, adjust your power, and clear the table! Avoid scratching (pocketing the cue ball) as it's a foul. Progress through increasingly challenging levels to master your pool skills!

Arrow Left/Right: Rotate cue stick to aim (hold for continuous)
Arrow Up/Down: Adjust shot power (hold for continuous)
Shift + Arrow Keys: Apply spin (Up=top, Down=back, Left/Right=side)
Space: Execute shot
Z: Confirm cue ball placement (after foul)
Enter: Start game
Escape: Pause/Unpause
R: Restart to main menu
