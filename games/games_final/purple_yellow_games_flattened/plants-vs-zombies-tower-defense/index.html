Plants vs Zombies - Tower Defense

Defend your lawn from waves of approaching zombies! Plant sunflowers to generate sun resources, then use sun to plant offensive units like peashooters. Zombies march down 5 lanes toward your house - if any reach the left side, you lose. Collect falling sun and strategically place plants to stop all zombies in each wave to win. Each plant has unique abilities and costs, so manage your resources wisely!

Arrow Keys (↑↓←→): Move plant placement cursor on lawn | Z: Switch plant selection | C: Collect falling sun (when near cursor) | Space: Place selected plant at cursor position | Shift+Space: Dig up plant | Enter: Start game from title screen | ESC: Pause/unpause game | R: Restart game (from game over screen)
