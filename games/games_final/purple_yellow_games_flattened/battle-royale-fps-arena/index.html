Battle Royale FPS Arena

Battle Royale FPS Arena: You are a soldier in a competitive arena match. Eliminate enemy soldiers to earn XP and unlock weapon upgrades. Survive waves of increasingly skilled opponents while collecting ammo and health packs. Reach level 5 to win the match! Watch your health and ammo carefully.

Arrow Keys: HOLD to move/aim continuously (UP/DOWN to move, LEFT/RIGHT to aim) | Space: TAP to shoot weapon | Shift (hold): Sprint mode (faster movement) | Z: TAP to reload weapon | Enter: Start game | Esc: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart to main menu
