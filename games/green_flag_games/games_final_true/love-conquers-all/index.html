Love Conquers All

An alien girl uses love to defeat bullet-hell bosses. Collect power-ups to strengthen her heart, conquer stages, and face The Void.

Arrow Keys: Move your heart around the arena | Z Key: Fire love projectiles at enemies | Shift Key: Focus mode (slower movement, shows hitbox) | Space Key: Use special ability (if unlocked) | ENTER: Start game from title screen | ESC: Pause/Unpause during gameplay | R: Restart game (from game over screen)
