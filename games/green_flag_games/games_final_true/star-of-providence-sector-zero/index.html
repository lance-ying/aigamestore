Star of Providence: Sector Zero is a fast-paced top-down shooter. Pilot your ship through procedurally generated sectors, battling foes and upgrading your weaponry. Clear 5 sectors to defeat the Guardian and secure the Providence Core.

Arrow Keys: Move your ship. | Z: Fire weapon. | Space: Dash (evasive maneuver with temporary invulnerability). | Shift: Focus Mode (Slow movement, lock facing direction for precision strafing). | Enter: Start Game. | Esc: Pause / Unpause. | R: Restart Game (from Game Over screens).
