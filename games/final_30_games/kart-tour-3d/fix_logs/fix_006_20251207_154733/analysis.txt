- Issue identified:
    1.  **Car Tilting**: The cars are initialized with `lookAt` targeting a point on the ground (y=0) while the cars are spawned in the air (y=1), causing them to pitch downward permanently.
    2.  **Wheel Clipping**: The ride height logic in `Kart.update` targets a Y position that places the wheel geometry slightly below the road surface.
    3.  **Slow Speed**: The `maxSpeed` and `acceleration` values are too low for the desired gameplay feel.
    4.  **No Collisions**: There is no logic to handle collisions between karts, allowing them to pass through each other.
- Files affected: `game.js`, `entities.js`
- Changes needed:
    -   **`game.js`**:
        -   Fix `setupGame` to make karts look at a point at their own height (y+1) instead of the ground (y=0) to prevent pitch tilt.
        -   Add a `checkCollisions` function and call it in the `gameLoop` to handle kart-to-kart bumping and separation.
    -   **`entities.js`**:
        -   Increase `maxSpeed` and `acceleration` in `Kart` constructor.
        -   Add `bumpVelocity` vector to `Kart` to handle collision impulses.
        -   Update `Kart.update` to apply and dampen `bumpVelocity`.
        -   Increase the target Y offset in the ground clamping logic to raise the chassis so wheels sit on top of the road.
