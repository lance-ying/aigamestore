Ocean Hunter: Cannon Fury

Ocean Hunter: Cannon Fury is an arcade shooter where you control a powerful cannon at the ocean floor, defending against waves of sea creatures. Your mission is to hunt fish across 9 challenging levels - 3 easy, 3 medium, and 3 hard levels, each with increasing difficulty. Destroy different types of fish to earn points - from small sardines to massive boss creatures like sharks and the legendary Giant Squid. Complete each level by reaching the target score before time runs out. Master your aim and timing to become the ultimate ocean hunter!

Arrow Left (←): Rotate cannon counter-clockwise
Arrow Right (→): Rotate cannon clockwise
Space: Fire projectile
ENTER: Start game from menu
ESC: Pause/Unpause during gameplay
R: Return to menu from game over screen
