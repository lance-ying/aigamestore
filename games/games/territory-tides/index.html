Territory Tides

Territory Tides is a turn-based strategy game where you must conquer territories to dominate the map. Each turn, you'll reinforce your armies, launch attacks on enemy territories, and fortify your borders. Capture all territories to advance through increasingly challenging levels!

CONTROLS (Keyboard Only):
Arrow Left/Right: Navigate territories | Enter: Select highlighted territory | Arrow Up/Down: Adjust army count | Space: Confirm action | Shift: Next phase | Z: End turn | Esc: Pause | R: Restart (from game over)
