TEMPLE RUN

You've stolen the cursed idol! Now run for your life through an endless ancient temple. Swipe left and right to turn corners, jump over gaps, and slide under obstacles. Collect golden coins to increase your score. The temple generates endlessly – how far can you run before the relentless Demon Monkey catches you? Your speed increases over time, making survival even more challenging!

Controls: Arrow Left: Turn left | Arrow Right: Turn right | Arrow Up/Space: Jump | Arrow Down: Slide | ESC: Pause | R: Restart
