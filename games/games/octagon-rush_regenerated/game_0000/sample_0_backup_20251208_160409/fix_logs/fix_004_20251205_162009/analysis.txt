- Issue identified: 
    1. "Invisible obstacle" deaths are caused by the collision logic checking the player's new position (ceiling/floor) immediately when a flip starts, while the visual animation is still in progress (mid-air). This causes the player to die from obstacles on the destination surface before visually reaching it.
    2. "Tiles disappearing" is caused by aggressive culling of tunnel segments. Segments were being removed when `z < -100` and stopped rendering when `z < -200`, causing them to vanish while still potentially visible or just as they passed the player, creating a popping effect.
- Files affected: `physics.js`, `game.js`, `entities.js`
- Changes needed:
    1. In `physics.js`, disable collision detection when `player.isFlipping` is true. This makes the player effectively "jump" between surfaces without hitting walls in mid-air, matching the visual expectation.
    2. In `game.js`, change the segment removal threshold from `-100` to `-500` to keep segments in memory longer as they pass behind the camera.
    3. In `entities.js`, change the render culling threshold from `-200` to `-500` to ensure segments remain visible until they are well behind the view.
