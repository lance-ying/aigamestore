- Issue identified: The game loop fails to transition to `endPlayerTurn` after a match cascade completes. This prevents the enemy death check (which happens in `endPlayerTurn`) from running, leaving the enemy at 0 HP indefinitely. It also prevents the enemy from attacking, so the player takes no damage.
