A Dark Room

You wake in a cold, dark room with only embers remaining. Your survival depends on gathering wood to keep the fire alive. As warmth returns, strangers appear and your settlement grows. Build structures to automate resource gathering, unlock hunting and trapping, then venture into dangerous expeditions to uncover the truth. Reach the ancient city to complete your journey.

Arrow Keys: Navigate menu options and expedition map | Space: Select/confirm action or move forward in expedition | Shift: Cancel/go back in menus | Z: Quick-select first available action | Enter: Start game | Esc: Pause/unpause | R: Return to start screen
