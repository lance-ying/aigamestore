Life Journey

Experience a streamlined single-player journey through life's key decisions! Spin the spinner to move along a winding path, making strategic choices at career, education, and investment spaces. Land on event spaces to trigger quick tap-based minigames where success earns bonus cash. Accumulate wealth through smart decisions and skillful play. Reach the Retirement space with the highest net worth to win, but watch out—poor choices can lead to bankruptcy and game over!

Arrow Keys (↑/↓ or ←/→): Navigate menu choices | Space: Spin spinner / Confirm / Tap in minigames | Z: Alternative action in minigames | Enter: Start game | Esc: Pause/unpause | R: Restart
