EARN TO DIE: Zombie Wasteland

Drive through a zombie apocalypse wasteland, covering maximum distance while destroying zombies. Your vehicle automatically accelerates with limited fuel and nitro. Survive by managing your resources, avoiding damage, and earning cash to upgrade your vehicle between runs. Reach checkpoints to progress through increasingly difficult terrain filled with more zombies and obstacles.

Controls: Arrow Up/Space: Activate nitro boost | Arrow Down: Brake/Reverse | Arrow Left/Right: Control vehicle pitch in air | ENTER: Start game | ESC: Pause/Unpause | R: Return to start screen
