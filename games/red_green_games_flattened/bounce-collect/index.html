Bounce & Collect

Drop balls from the top of the screen to bounce through a peg-filled board, aiming to hit multiplier gates that duplicate your balls. Each ball lands in a collection bin at the bottom, earning points based on the bin's value. Complete levels by reaching target scores within limited drops. Strategic placement and timing are key to maximizing your score through multipliers!

LEFT ARROW: Move drop position left
RIGHT ARROW: Move drop position right
SPACE: Drop ball at current position
ENTER: Start game from start screen
ESC: Pause/Unpause game
R: Restart game (from game over screen)
