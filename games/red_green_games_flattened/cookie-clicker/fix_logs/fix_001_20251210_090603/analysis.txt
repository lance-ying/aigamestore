- Issue identified: The "Cursor" building has a `baseCps` of 0.1, meaning it generates 0.1 cookies per second. However, the `formatNumber` utility function, used for displaying cookie counts and CPS, uses `Math.floor()` for numbers less than 1000. This causes 0.1 to be floored to 0, making it appear as if the Cursor adds "0 per second" in the UI, even though it's producing cookies internally.
- Files affected: `rendering.js`
