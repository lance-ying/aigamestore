Rhythm Flow Arcade

Welcome to Rhythm Flow Arcade! Match the rhythm by pressing the correct keys as colored notes flow down the screen. Hit notes precisely when they reach the hit zone at the bottom to score points and build combos. Maintain your life bar by hitting notes accurately - Perfect and Great hits fill it up, while misses drain it quickly. Survive through 5 increasingly challenging levels, each with faster tempos and more complex patterns. Master the timing, build massive combos, and achieve the highest score!

A: Hit notes in Lane 1 (leftmost, red) | S: Hit notes in Lane 2 (left-center, blue) | D: Hit notes in Lane 3 (right-center, green) | W: Hit notes in Lane 4 (rightmost, yellow) | ENTER: Start game from menu | ESC: Pause/Unpause during gameplay | R: Return to main menu (from game over or pause) | SPACE: Confirm menu selections
