Grid Tactics: 3-in-a-Row

Grid Tactics: 3-in-a-Row is a turn-based strategy game where you compete against an AI opponent to get three marks in a row. Navigate the grid with arrow keys and place your X marks strategically. Progress through 5 increasingly challenging levels with growing grid sizes (3x3 to 11x11) and smarter AI opponents. Win by forming a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal line of three X's before the AI gets three O's. Earn points for wins, draws, and bonuses for speed and difficulty!

Arrow Keys: Navigate the grid to select a cell | Space: Place your X mark in the selected cell | Enter: Start game from menu / Confirm menu selections | Escape: Pause game during play | R: Restart and return to main menu | Shift: Quick restart level (from game over screen)
