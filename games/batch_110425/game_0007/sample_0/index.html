Dragon Quest Builders

Welcome to Dragon Quest Builders! You are a legendary builder tasked with restoring the ruined world of Alefgard. Explore a block-based world, gather materials by breaking blocks, craft tools and items, and construct buildings to complete quests. Defend your settlement from monster attacks and unlock new recipes as you progress. Complete quests to restore the land and prove yourself as the ultimate builder!

Controls: Arrow Keys: Move player around the world | Space: Break block (hold to break, releases materials) / Attack enemy | Z: Place block from inventory / Interact with NPCs | Shift: Open/Close crafting menu (when available) | Enter: Start game | Esc: Pause/Unpause game | R: Restart game (return to start screen)
