Only You Are Here

You awaken in a surreal, fragmented world where memories and reality blur. Navigate through mysterious rooms, solve environmental puzzles, and collect memory fragments to piece together your story. Beware of the lurking Shadow Entity that hunts you in certain areas. Your choices matter—discover secrets, unlock different paths, and reach one of multiple endings. Will you uncover the truth, or remain lost forever?

Controls: Arrow Keys: Move forward/backward and turn left/right | Space: Interact with objects | Shift: Sprint | Z: Use collected items | ESC: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart | ENTER: Start game
