Knight and Dragon 2: Age of Madness

Lead a party of four heroes through a hack-and-slash RPG adventure! Your heroes automatically attack nearby enemies in real-time combat. Activate powerful character skills using number keys (1-4) to turn the tide of battle. Defeat enemies to collect randomized loot and gain experience. Equipment is automatically equipped to boost your stats. Progress through multiple story missions, unlock higher classes for your heroes, and survive the infinite dungeon for endless challenges!

Arrow Keys: Move party leader (all party members follow) | Space: Dash forward quickly | Z: Use special party-wide ability (when charged) | 1-4: Activate hero-specific skills (with cooldowns) | Enter: Start game from menu | ESC: Pause/Unpause game | R: Return to start screen
