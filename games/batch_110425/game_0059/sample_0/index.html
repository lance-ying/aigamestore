君しかいない - You Are The Only One

Navigate through a dark, mysterious facility as the sole survivor. Explore interconnected rooms, examine objects for clues, and solve environmental puzzles to uncover the truth. Find keycards to unlock new areas, read journal entries to piece together the story, and discover one of multiple endings. Beware: wrong choices or dangerous encounters can lead to immediate game over. Your goal is to escape by finding all necessary items and making the right decisions.

Controls: Arrow Keys: Move forward/backward and turn left/right | Space: Interact with objects | Shift: Sprint | Z: Use equipped keycard at doors | ESC: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart | Enter: Start game
