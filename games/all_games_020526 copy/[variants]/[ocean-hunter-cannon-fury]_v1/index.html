Control a cannon at the ocean floor, defending against waves of sea creatures. Hunt fish across challenging levels, destroying them to earn points. Reach the target score before time runs out to become the ultimate!

Arrow Left (←): Rotate cannon counter-clockwise | Arrow Right (→): Rotate cannon clockwise | Space: Fire projectile | ENTER: Start game from menu | ESC: Pause/Unpause during gameplay | R: Return to menu from game over screen
