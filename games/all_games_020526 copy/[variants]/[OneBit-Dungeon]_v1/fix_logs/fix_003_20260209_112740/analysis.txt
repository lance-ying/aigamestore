- Files affected: `game.js`

  1. Modify the `p.draw` loop in `game.js` to stop advancing the game's internal frame counter (`gameState.frameCount`) and delta time when the game is in the `PAUSED` state. This ensures that animations (like item bobbing) truly freeze.
