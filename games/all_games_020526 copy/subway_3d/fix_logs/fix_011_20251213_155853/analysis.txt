- Issue identified: The game score was being overwritten every frame in `physics.js` based solely on the distance traveled (`gameState.score = Math.floor(gameState.distance)`). This meant that although `entities.js` attempted to add 10 points to the score when a coin was collected, this addition was immediately discarded by the distance-based overwrite in the next update cycle.
- Files affected: `physics.js`, `entities.js`
- Changes needed:
  1.  In `physics.js`: Modify the score calculation to explicitly include the coin score. The new formula will be `gameState.score = Math.floor(gameState.distance) + (gameState.coins_collected * 10)`. This ensures that every collected coin adds 10 points to the total score permanently.
  2.  In `entities.js`: Remove the `gameState.score += 10` line from the `Coin` class's `collect` method. Since the score is now derived from `coins_collected` in the main loop, manually adding to `score` here is redundant and would be overwritten anyway.
