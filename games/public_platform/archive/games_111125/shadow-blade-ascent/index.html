Shadow Blade Ascent

Shadow Blade Ascent is a 2D side-scrolling action RPG where you control a shadow knight through 5 increasingly challenging levels. Battle waves of shadow enemies, defeat powerful bosses, and level up your character to unlock new skills. Collect gold for points, use health and mana potions strategically, and master your combat abilities including attacks, dodges, and special skills. Your goal is to defeat all 5 bosses and conquer the Dark Lord's Throne Room!

Arrow Left/Right: Move left/right (hold to keep moving) | Arrow Up: Jump | Arrow Down: Drop through platform | Z: Basic Attack | Shift: Dodge/Dash (grants brief invulnerability) | Space: Energy Burst (powerful AoE attack around you) | W: Magic Projectile (ranged attack, unlocked at level 2) | ENTER: Start game | ESC: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart (return to start screen)
