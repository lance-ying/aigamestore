Pixel Kick: Dream League

Score goals in this top-down soccer challenge! Aim your shot, adjust power, and apply curve to beat defenders and the goalkeeper. Progress through 5 increasingly difficult levels, each requiring precision and skill. Master the curve mechanic to bend shots around obstacles. Complete all levels to become the champion!

Arrow Keys: Adjust aim direction (Left/Right) and power (Up/Down) | Z: Apply left curve/spin to the ball | Shift: Apply right curve/spin to the ball | Space: Take the shot with current settings | Enter: Start game from main menu | Escape: Pause/unpause during gameplay | R: Return to main menu from game over screen
