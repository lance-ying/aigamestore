- Issue identified: The collision detection logic uses the player's exact corners without padding. When the player is moving horizontally on the ground, the bottom corners of the player's bounding box align exactly with the top of the floor tiles. This causes the collision system to interpret the floor tiles as a vertical wall in the horizontal path, snapping the player's X position to the left side of the floor tile (teleporting them left). A similar issue can occur with vertical movement against walls.
- Files affected: player.js
- Changes needed: Update `handleCollisions` in `player.js` to apply a small "padding" inset to the collision check points. When checking horizontal movement, inset the Y coordinates to avoid catching the floor/ceiling. When checking vertical movement, inset the X coordinates to avoid catching walls.
