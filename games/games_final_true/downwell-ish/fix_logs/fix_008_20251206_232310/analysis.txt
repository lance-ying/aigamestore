- Issue identified: 
    1. The player falls too fast, making the game feel heavy.
    2. When jumping from the ground, the "gunboots" (shooting) trigger immediately because the jump key (Space) remains held down during the transition from ground to air, causing accidental ammo usage.
- Files affected: `globals.js`, `entities.js`
- Changes needed:
    1. In `globals.js`: Reduce `gravity` from 0.6 to 0.45 to slow down the fall speed.
    2. In `entities.js`: Update the `Player` class to include a `didJump` flag. This flag is set to `true` when a jump is initiated from the ground. The shooting logic now checks this flag and prevents shooting if the current key press was responsible for the jump. The flag resets when the Space key is released, requiring a fresh press (or a release-and-press) to shoot after jumping.
