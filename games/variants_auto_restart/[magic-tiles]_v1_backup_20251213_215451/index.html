A rhythm game where you tap black tiles as they scroll down, avoiding white ones. Hit tiles to score and build combos; miss or tap wrong and you lose. The game speeds up, testing your reflexes.

DOWN ARROW: Tap leftmost lane | LEFT ARROW: Tap second lane | UP ARROW: Tap third lane | RIGHT ARROW: Tap rightmost lane | ENTER: Start game | ESC: Pause/unpause | R: Restart
