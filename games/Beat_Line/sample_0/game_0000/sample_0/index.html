Beat Line

Guide a continuously moving line through rhythmic paths filled with obstacles. Time your turns perfectly to the beat of the music as you navigate increasingly challenging tracks. Master the rhythm, avoid collisions, and complete all three levels to prove your skills. Each perfect turn builds your combo and boosts your score!

SPACE: Execute 90-degree turn (tap to the beat!) | ESC: Pause/Unpause game | ENTER: Start game / Confirm menu selection | R: Restart (return to start screen) | Arrow Keys: Navigate menus
