Disney Solitaire

Clear all cards from the tableau in this Disney-themed Tripeaks Solitaire! Match cards that are one rank higher or lower than the top discard card. Create chains for bonus points. Complete all three levels with unique Disney-inspired backgrounds to win. Use your power-up wisely when you're stuck!

ENTER: Start the game | ESC: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart (return to start screen) | Arrow Keys: Navigate between selectable cards and UI elements | Space: Select/Play highlighted card or draw from stock | Z: Activate power-up (one per level)
