So the problem is that all 9 levels' obstacles are being created at initialization and stored in shared global arrays, then ALL of them are being rendered and used for collision detection regardless of which hole is current.

2. Only render and check collisions for the CURRENT hole's obstacles

4. Modify Wall, WaterHazard, Ramp constructors to take a hole parameter
