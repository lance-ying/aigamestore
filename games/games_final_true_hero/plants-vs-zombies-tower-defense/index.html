Defend your lawn from zombie waves! Plant unique units using sun to stop them before they reach your house. Strategize plant placement and resource management to win each wave.

Arrow Keys (↑↓←→): Move plant placement cursor on lawn | Z: Switch plant selection | C: Collect falling sun (when near cursor) | Space: Place selected plant at cursor position | Shift+Space: Dig up plant | Enter: Start game from title screen | ESC: Pause/unpause game | R: Restart game (from game over screen)
