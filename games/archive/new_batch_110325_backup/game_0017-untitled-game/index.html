ROCKMAN X: Deep Log Protocol

Navigate through the Deep Log cyberworld as a legendary reploid warrior. Battle through waves of corrupted enemies across multiple stages, collecting energy crystals and power-ups to enhance your abilities. Defeat the stage boss to complete each level and progress deeper into the corrupted data. Survive enemy attacks while managing your health and energy to achieve 100% stage completion.

Arrow Keys: Move left/right, aim weapon up/down | Space: Jump (press twice for double jump) | Z: Fire primary weapon (auto-aims at nearest enemy) | Shift: Dash forward (requires energy) | R: Restart game (from game over screen) | Enter: Start game | ESC: Pause/Unpause
