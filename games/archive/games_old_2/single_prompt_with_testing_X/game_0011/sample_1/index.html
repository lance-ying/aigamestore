Mob Control

Command your cannon to fire waves of units up multi-lane battlefields! Strategically aim through multiplier gates (x2, x3, +50) to amplify your forces before they clash with enemies. Deploy powerful Champion units to break through defenses. Your mission: destroy the enemy base (500 HP) before they destroy yours. Earn Championship Stars and unlock card packs to upgrade your units and dominate higher leagues!

Controls: Arrow Left/Right: Aim cannon | Space: Fire units (hold) | Z: Deploy Champion (100 units) | Shift: Speed Boost (50 units) | Enter: Start | ESC: Pause | R: Restart
