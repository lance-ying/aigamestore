Hill Climb Racing

Race uphill with Bill in this physics-based driving challenge! Navigate treacherous terrain without crashing or running out of fuel. Collect coins to boost your score and reach the finish line. Master the balance between speed and control as you tackle steep hills, valleys, and jumps. Complete the course to win!

Arrow Right / D: Accelerate | Arrow Left / A: Brake/Reverse | Space: Balance control (shift weight forward) | Enter: Start game | ESC: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart (from game over screen)
