Terraria-Like Adventure

Explore a procedurally generated 2D world where you mine resources, craft better tools, build structures, and battle enemies. Progress through tiers of equipment by defeating bosses that unlock new materials and areas. Survive the dangerous nights when more enemies spawn, and work toward crafting the ultimate gear to defeat the final boss and win the game.

Arrow Keys: Move character left/right and jump | Z: Mine/Attack (hold to charge for stronger attack) | SPACE: Place blocks from inventory | Shift: Open/Close crafting menu | ENTER: Start game | ESC: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart game
