Frost Survival: Last City

Lead the last city of survivors in a frozen apocalypse! Manage resources (food, wood, coal), construct buildings, and recruit heroes to defend against beasts. Balance resource gathering with city expansion while protecting your citizens from the harsh winter and hostile creatures. Survive 10 waves of increasingly difficult beast attacks to prove your city is the strongest and win the game. Your city starts with basic resources - use them wisely to build a thriving civilization before winter claims you all.

Arrow Keys: Navigate and select buildings/heroes | Space: Confirm selection / Build / Recruit / Upgrade | Z: Cancel current action / Deselect | Shift: Speed up time (hold) | Enter: Start game | ESC: Pause/Unpause game | R: Restart (return to start screen)
