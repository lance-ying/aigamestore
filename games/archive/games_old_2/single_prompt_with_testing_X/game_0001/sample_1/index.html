Lep's Adventure

Guide Lep the leprechaun through magical platforming worlds! Navigate treacherous levels filled with enemies, collect shimmering gold coins for points, and gather healing cloverleaves to restore your health. Jump on enemy heads to defeat them, but beware of falling into pits or losing all your health. Reach the flag at the end of each level to progress through increasingly challenging stages across mystical realms.

A/D Keys: Move left/right | W Key: Jump (tap for normal jump, hold for higher jump) | Arrow Keys: Alternative movement (←/→ move, ↑ jumps) | Space: Jump | Enter: Start game | Escape: Pause/unpause | R: Restart (when game over)
