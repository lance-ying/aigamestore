黄金矿工 - Gold Miner

You are a gold miner trying to reach money targets within time limits! Control a swinging claw that automatically moves left and right at the top of the mine. Deploy the claw to grab valuable items from below - gold nuggets, diamonds, and rocks. Each item has different values and weights affecting how fast the claw retracts. Meet the level target before time runs out to advance. Between levels, visit the shop to buy power-ups like dynamite to destroy unwanted items or strength potions to speed up retraction. Progress through increasingly challenging levels with higher targets!

SPACE: Deploy/release the claw to grab items
D: Use dynamite (destroys currently grabbed item, purchased from shop)
S: Use strength potion (speeds up claw retraction, purchased from shop)
ENTER: Start game / Continue to next level / Enter shop
ESC: Pause/Unpause game
R: Restart (from game over screen)
