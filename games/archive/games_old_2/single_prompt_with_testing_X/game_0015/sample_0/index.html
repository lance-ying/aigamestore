Red Wrecker

Red Wrecker is a physics-based puzzle game where you must eliminate all red shapes while keeping all green shapes safely on the platform. Click on shapes to remove them from the game, causing the remaining pieces to fall and settle according to realistic physics. Complete all 100 levels by clearing every red shape without letting any green shape fall off the platform. Carefully plan your moves - you have limited clicks per level!

Controls: MOUSE CLICK: Remove a shape | ENTER: Start the game | ESC: Pause/unpause | R: Restart | SPACE: Next level (when won)
