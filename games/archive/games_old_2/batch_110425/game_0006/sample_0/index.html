THE PAST WITHIN

"The Past Within" is a cooperative puzzle adventure where two players collaborate across time. One player explores a 2D Past environment while another navigates a 3D Future environment. Players must communicate externally to share clues, solve interconnected puzzles, and progress through 2 chapters. Click on objects to examine or interact with them, and use information from your partner to unlock mechanisms in your timeline. Complete all puzzles in both chapters to win!

Controls: Arrow Keys: Select objects | Space: Interact | Z: Use item | Shift: Switch timeline | Enter: Start | Esc: Pause | R: Restart
