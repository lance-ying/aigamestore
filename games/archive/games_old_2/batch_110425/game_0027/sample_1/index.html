Waterpark Story

Build and manage your dream water park! Place pools, slides, and facilities on a grid to attract guests. Serve food and drinks at restaurants, and gift items like tubes and swimsuits to keep visitors happy. Satisfied guests become SNS followers and spread the word about your park. Earn money from guests, unlock new attractions, and expand your park to create the ultimate water paradise. Balance construction costs with guest satisfaction to achieve a 5-star rating!

Controls:
Arrow Keys: Navigate menu and grid selection
Space: Confirm selection / Place item / Speed up time
Z: Cancel / Delete selected tile
Shift: Toggle between Build/Gift/Delete modes
R: Restart game (from game over screen)
Enter: Start game (from start screen)
ESC: Pause/Unpause game
