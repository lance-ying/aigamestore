Lux DLX 3

Lux DLX 3 is a turn-based strategy game where you compete against an AI opponent to conquer all territories on the map. Each turn, receive reinforcements based on your controlled territories, deploy armies to your regions, and attack adjacent enemy territories. Combat is resolved through dice rolls - you need at least 3 armies to attack. Conquer at least one territory per turn to earn bonus cards that can be traded for extra reinforcements. Victory is achieved by controlling all territories on the map.

Arrow Keys: Navigate/select territories during deployment and attack phases | Space: Confirm selection (deploy armies, initiate attack, trade cards) | Z: End current phase (deployment/attack/fortification) | ENTER: Start game from start screen | ESC: Pause/unpause game | R: Return to start screen
