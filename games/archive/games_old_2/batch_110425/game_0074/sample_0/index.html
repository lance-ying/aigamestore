☕ Cafe Master Story

Manage your dream coffee shop in Cafe Master Story! Place furniture to boost atmosphere, research recipes by combining ingredients, and serve customers to earn revenue and popularity. Satisfy customers to unlock regulars and higher-tier demographics. Expand your cafe, participate in Combo Meal contests, and aim for a Five-Star rating. Reach popularity milestones to relocate to new towns with unique challenges!

Controls: Arrow Keys: Navigate menus | Space: Confirm/Place/Serve | Shift: Open/Close menu | Z: Cancel/Back | Enter: Start | ESC: Pause | R: Restart
