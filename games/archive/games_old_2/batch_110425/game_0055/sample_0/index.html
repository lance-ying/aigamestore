ICEY - Meta-Narrative Combat

You are ICEY, a cybernetic warrior fighting through waves of enemies in a mysterious facility. Battle through combat arenas, defeat enemies with fluid combos, and face off against powerful bosses. But there's a twist - a narrator guides your path. Will you follow their instructions, or defy them to uncover hidden secrets and alternate endings? Your choices shape the narrative as you fight for survival.

Controls: Arrow Keys: Move/Jump | Space: Light Attack | Shift: Heavy Attack | Z: Dash | Enter: Start | ESC: Pause | R: Restart
