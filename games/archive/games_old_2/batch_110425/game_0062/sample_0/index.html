Mindustry Lite

Build your factory, defend your core! Extract resources with drills, transport them via conveyors, and refine materials in factories. Use resources to build defensive turrets and units. Enemy waves attack every 45 seconds with increasing strength. Survive 10 waves to win, or lose if your core is destroyed. Plan your factory layout carefully to maximize production and defense!

Arrow Keys: Move camera view | Space: Place selected building at cursor position | Z: Delete building at cursor position | Shift: Cycle through available buildings (Drill → Conveyor → Factory → Turret → Unit Factory)
