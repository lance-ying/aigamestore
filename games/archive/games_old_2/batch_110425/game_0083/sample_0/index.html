Refind Self

Welcome to "Refind Self," a personality diagnosis adventure! You are a robot exploring a mysterious facility filled with other robots. Move around, interact with characters, solve simple puzzles, and make dialogue choices. Every action you take is analyzed to build your unique personality profile. There are no wrong choices—just be yourself! Complete the game to discover your personality type, then play again to unlock new paths and profiles.

Controls: Arrow Keys: Move | Space: Interact, advance dialogue | Z: Alternative interact | ESC: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart | ENTER: Start game
