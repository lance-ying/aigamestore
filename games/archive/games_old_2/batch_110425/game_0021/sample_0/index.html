Dark Kingdom Tower Defense

Defend your dark kingdom against waves of heroic invaders! Strategically place powerful towers along enemy paths, upgrade them during battle, and deploy your hero to turn the tide. Earn gold to strengthen defenses, collect stars for permanent upgrades, and survive increasingly challenging waves. Prevent 20 enemies from reaching the end or face defeat!

Arrow Keys: Move hero | Space: Place/Select tower | Z: Upgrade tower or hero ability | Shift: Sell tower | Enter: Start game | ESC: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart
