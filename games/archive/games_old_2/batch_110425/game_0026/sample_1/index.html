ゆうえんち夢物語 (Amusement Park Story)

Welcome to ゆうえんち夢物語 (Amusement Park Story)! Build and manage your dream amusement park by placing attractions on a grid to attract guests. Guests generate income and satisfaction points that unlock new attractions and upgrades through research. Expand your park's land area, recruit mascots to unlock themed zones, and climb the national park rankings. Balance guest satisfaction, income generation, and strategic expansion to become the top-ranked amusement park in the nation!

Controls: Arrow Keys: Navigate menu/camera | Space: Open menu/Confirm | Z: Cancel/Remove | Shift: Fast-forward (hold) | Enter: Start | Esc: Pause | R: Restart
