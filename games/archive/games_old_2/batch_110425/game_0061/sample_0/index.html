海姆达尔 HEIMDALLR

You are Jason, a hacker investigating a 15-year-old murder case. Navigate through alternating puzzle and parkour segments to uncover the truth. In puzzle mode, decrypt codes and combine items to reveal clues. In parkour mode, run, jump, and slide through dangerous environments. Collect data fragments to unlock different story endings. Complete all chapters to solve the mystery!

Controls: Arrow Keys: Navigate menus, move left/right | Space: Jump, Select/Interact | Z: Slide | Shift: Sprint | Enter: Start | Esc: Pause | R: Restart
