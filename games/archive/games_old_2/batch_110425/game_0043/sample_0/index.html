タイムサーファー (Time Surfer)

Surf across cosmic platforms while collecting gems and avoiding obstacles! The cosmic end chases you from the left - stay ahead or perish. Dive to gain speed down slopes, release to rise up. Use your limited time rewind ability to undo mistakes and escape danger. Collect gems to increase your score and survive as long as possible!

SPACE: Hold to dive (gain speed), Release to rise | Z: Activate time rewind (costs time energy) | ENTER: Start game | ESC: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart to main menu
