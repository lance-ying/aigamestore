Territory Tides

Territory Tides is a turn-based strategy game where you must conquer territories to dominate the map. Each turn, you'll reinforce your armies, launch attacks on enemy territories, and fortify your borders. Capture all territories to advance through increasingly challenging levels. Control continents for bonus armies and eliminate AI opponents to achieve victory!

Arrow Keys: Navigate and select territories (in combination with clicks) | Arrow Up/Down: Adjust army count for deployment, attack, or fortification | Space: Confirm actions (deploy armies, attack, fortify) | Shift: Advance to next phase of your turn | Z: Skip remaining phases and end your turn | Enter: Start game from menu | Esc: Pause/unpause game | R: Return to main menu from game over screen
