Happy Hoof Care

Welcome to Happy Hoof Care! You're managing a zoo where adorable animals need your care. Play mini-games to keep your animals happy by shaving alpacas, showering elephants, guiding them through mazes, and feeding giraffes. Successfully complete mini-games to increase animal happiness and earn points. Progress through 5 levels by completing enough mini-games while keeping your animals happy. If any animal's happiness drops to 0% or you fail too many mini-games, it's game over. Can you become the ultimate Zoo Tycoon?

Arrow Keys: Navigate menus, move cursor/tool in mini-games, control animal in maze
Space: Confirm menu selections, activate tools (hold to shave/shower/spray), grab/release food
Shift: Go back to previous menu, cancel actions
Enter: Start the game from main menu
ESC: Pause/unpause during gameplay
R: Restart and return to main menu from game over screen
