Hogwarts Spellbound: The Cursed Scrolls

Welcome to Hogwarts Spellbound: The Cursed Scrolls! You are a young wizard investigating the mystery of the Cursed Vaults at Hogwarts. Progress through three years of magical education by completing tasks, casting spells in mini-games, and making crucial dialogue choices. Manage your energy wisely, develop your attributes (Courage, Empathy, Knowledge), and unravel the secrets hidden within the castle walls. Complete all chapters to solve the mystery and graduate as a true magical hero!

ENTER: Start the game from the main menu | SPACE: Advance dialogue, confirm choices, perform Quick-Time Events (QTE) | ARROW UP/DOWN: Navigate dialogue options and menu choices | W/A/S/D: Directional inputs for spell tracing mini-games | ESC: Pause/Unpause the game | R: Restart and return to main menu from Game Over screen
