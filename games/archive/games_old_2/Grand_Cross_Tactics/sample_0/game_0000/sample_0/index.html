Grand Cross Tactics

Grand Cross Tactics is a turn-based tactical card battle game. Command your hero through 5 challenging levels, each filled with increasingly dangerous enemies. During your turn, select and play ability cards to attack, defend, heal, and buff yourself. Manage your Health Points (HP) and Ability Points (AP) wisely to survive enemy assaults. Defeat all enemies in each level to progress. Clear all 5 levels to achieve victory!

ENTER: Start game from menu | LEFT/RIGHT ARROW: Select ability card during your turn | SPACE: Play selected ability card | SHIFT: End your turn early | ESC: Pause/Unpause game | R: Restart to menu (from game over or win screen)
