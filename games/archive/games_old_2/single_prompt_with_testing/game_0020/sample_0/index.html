Beat the Boss

Battle through 7 increasingly challenging boss stages in this action-packed damage dealer! Unleash a massive arsenal of unique weapons to deplete each boss's health bar. Earn currency from hits and by completing humorous objectives to unlock 80+ weapons and upgrade their damage. Each weapon features distinct physics-based reactions and animations. Strategically manage your arsenal as bosses grow tougher with more health and resistance!

SPACE: Attack with current weapon | LEFT/RIGHT ARROW: Switch between unlocked weapons | UP ARROW: Purchase weapon upgrade (in shop) | DOWN ARROW: Purchase new weapon (in shop) | ENTER: Start game | ESC: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart to title screen
