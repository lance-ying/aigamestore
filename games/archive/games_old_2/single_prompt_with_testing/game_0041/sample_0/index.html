R-TYPE Space Fighter

Navigate your R-TYPE spaceship through intense waves of alien enemies across multiple levels. Manage your detachable Force pod - attach it for protection or launch it as an independent weapon. Collect power-ups to upgrade your weapons and abilities. Survive increasingly difficult enemy patterns and defeat the boss at the end of each level. Complete all levels with your limited lives to achieve victory.

Arrow Keys: Move spaceship in all directions | Space: Fire main weapon (hold to charge powerful beam shot) | Z: Detach/Attach Force pod | Shift: Launch Force pod forward | Enter: Start game | ESC: Pause/Unpause game | R: Restart from game over screen
