Wild Arena

Welcome to Wild Arena! You are a lone animal warrior fighting for survival in a shrinking battlefield. Battle waves of enemy animals across 5 increasingly challenging levels. Defeat all enemies and survive the timer while avoiding the deadly shrinking zone that closes in around you. Collect health packs, weapon upgrades, and ability boosts to stay alive. Master your special ability and prove you're the apex predator!

Arrow Keys: Move in 8 directions | Space: Fire your weapon | Shift: Activate special ability (Dash forward) | Enter: Start game / Confirm menu selections | Esc: Pause / Unpause game | R: Restart (return to start screen)
