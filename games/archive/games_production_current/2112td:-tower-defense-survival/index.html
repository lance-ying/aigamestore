2112TD: Tower Defense Survival

Defend your mobile command center against waves of alien invaders in this tower defense survival game. Deploy and upgrade towers (Machine Gun, Flamethrower, Cannon, Plasma) along a fixed path to destroy enemies before they reach your base. Each tower can be upgraded twice for enhanced power. Manage resources wisely and survive multiple waves to win. If any enemy reaches your command center, the game is over.

Arrow Keys: Navigate/Select towers and positions | Space: Place/Upgrade tower | Z: Cancel selection | Shift: Speed up (hold) | Enter: Start game | ESC: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart
