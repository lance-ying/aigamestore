Arena Reckoning: Pixel Horde

Arena Reckoning: Pixel Horde is a top-down survival auto-battler where you face endless waves of monsters across three increasingly challenging levels. Your character automatically attacks enemies while you focus on movement and strategic upgrade choices. Collect experience gems from defeated foes to level up and choose powerful upgrades. Survive timed waves, defeat mini-bosses and a final boss to claim victory. Each level ramps up difficulty with faster, stronger enemies and new threats. Master movement, make smart upgrade choices, and survive the pixel horde!

Arrow Keys / WASD: Move your character | Space: Confirm upgrade selection | Shift/ESC: Pause/Unpause game | Enter: Start game from main menu | R: Restart and return to main menu
