Cheese Chase: Mouse Escape

You are a clever mouse on a mission to collect all the delicious cheese scattered throughout increasingly dangerous environments! Navigate through 5 challenging levels filled with patrolling cats. Collect every piece of cheese to activate the mouse hole exit, then make your escape. But beware - one touch from a cat and you'll lose a life! Master the art of timing, precision jumping, and careful planning to complete all levels and claim victory.

Arrow Left: Hold to move left | Arrow Right: Hold to move right | Arrow Up: Jump (when on ground) | ENTER: Start game | ESC: Pause/Unpause game | R: Return to start screen
