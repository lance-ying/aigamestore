Shawarma Stack

Welcome to Shawarma Stack! You run a shawarma restaurant and must fulfill customer orders quickly and accurately. Each customer shows their order in a speech bubble - add the exact ingredients they request, then serve. Timely and perfect orders earn bonus points and maintain your reputation. If your reputation drops to 0% or you fail to meet level objectives, it's game over. Complete all 5 levels to become a Shawarma Legend!

Arrow Keys: Navigate menus and select sauces (Up: Garlic Sauce, Down: Tahini Sauce) | Z: Add Meat | Space: Serve Order | ENTER: Start Game / Confirm | ESC: Pause / Unpause | R: Restart (from Game Over screen) | Shift: Quick pause toggle during gameplay
