DEEMO - Rhythm of the Growing Tree

Guide the growth of a mystical tree by performing musical notes with precise timing. Notes descend from above and must be hit exactly when they reach the judgment line. Hold notes appear as long green bars - press and hold SPACE when the front reaches the line, then release after the entire bar passes through. Build combos for higher scores and faster tree growth!

Controls: Arrow Keys (←→): Move between lanes | Space: Hit notes / Hold long notes | Z: Release hold notes early | Enter: Start | Esc: Pause | R: Restart
