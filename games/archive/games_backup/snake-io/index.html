Snake.io

Guide your snake through three challenging levels, consuming glowing pellets and defeating AI opponents to grow longer. The arena wraps around like Pac-Man—go off one edge and appear on the opposite side! Avoid collisions with obstacles and other snakes. Reach the target length for each level to advance. Use your boost strategically to escape danger or chase opponents, but be careful—it costs you mass! Defeat AI snakes by making them crash into your body, then collect their remains to grow even larger.

Arrow Left/Right: Turn your snake left or right | Space or Shift: Activate speed boost (costs mass) | Enter: Start game or confirm menu selections | Escape: Pause/unpause game | R: Return to main menu from game over screen
