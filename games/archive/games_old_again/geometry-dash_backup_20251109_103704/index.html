Geometry Dash

Geometry Dash is a rhythm-based platformer where you control a geometric icon that automatically moves forward. Navigate through obstacles, spikes, and platforms by holding the jump key. Progress through 4 levels of increasing difficulty - complete each level to unlock the next challenge!

HOLD SPACE/UP ARROW: Jump (in cube mode) or fly (in ship mode)
ESC: Pause/unpause the game
R: Restart level (or advance after completing a level)

Hold the key to jump continuously in cube mode and fly in ship mode!
