Coffee Stack

Navigate your growing stack of coffee cups through a lane-based course! Collect empty cups, pass through gates to fill them with coffee, add sleeves, and attach lids to increase their value. Avoid obstacles that destroy cups from your stack. At the end, serve your customized cups to customers for coins. Earn more coins with larger, fully-customized stacks!

Arrow Left / A: Move stack to the left lane | Arrow Right / D: Move stack to the right lane | Enter: Start the game from the start screen | ESC: Pause/Unpause the game | R: Restart from game over screen
