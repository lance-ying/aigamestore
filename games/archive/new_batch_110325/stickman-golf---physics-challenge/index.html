Stickman Golf - Physics Challenge

Navigate your stickman golfer through challenging physics-based courses to sink the ball into the hole in the fewest strokes possible. Aim carefully and adjust your power to overcome obstacles like moving platforms, bouncy surfaces, and hazards. Complete courses to unlock new levels and earn currency to purchase power-ups. Each stroke counts toward your score—master the physics to achieve par or better!

Controls: Arrow Keys (Left/Right): Adjust shot angle | Arrow Keys (Up/Down): Adjust shot power | Space: Take the shot | S: Activate Sticky Ball power-up | W: Activate Power Boost power-up | Enter: Start game | Esc: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart
