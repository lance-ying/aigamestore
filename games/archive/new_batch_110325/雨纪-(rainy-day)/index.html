雨纪 (Rainy Day)

Navigate through a flooded isometric city. The water blocks your path—you can only move on accessible tiles. Activate light sources (lanterns, steles, crystals) to progressively lower the water level, revealing new paths. Each light you activate opens up more of the map. Collect cores for points and activate robots for bonuses. Your goal: reach the exit on each level.

Arrow Keys: Move one tile in any direction (only on accessible tiles) | Space: Activate light source or interact with object at your position | Shift: Skip to next level (testing only) | Enter: Start game | Esc: Pause/unpause | R: Restart from level 1
