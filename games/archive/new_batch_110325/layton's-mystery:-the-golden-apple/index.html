Layton's Mystery: The Golden Apple

Embark on a mystery adventure as Professor Layton! Navigate through a mysterious village, discover hidden puzzles, and interact with eccentric villagers. Solve logic puzzles to progress through the story and uncover the secret of the Golden Apple. Collect Hint Coins to help you when puzzles get tricky. Complete all mandatory puzzles to win!

Arrow Keys: Navigate between locations and move cursor in puzzle interface | Space: Interact with objects, NPCs, and confirm puzzle solutions | Z: Use a Hint Coin (when in puzzle mode) | Enter: Start game | ESC: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart game
