Vanderboom Legacy

Unravel the mysterious Vanderboom family history across generations. Explore intricate scenes, collect items, and solve interconnected puzzles to progress through the family tree. Each level represents a pivotal moment in the lineage. Interact with objects and use your inventory wisely to unlock secrets and advance the narrative. Complete all levels to reveal the full story.

Arrow Keys: Navigate between interactive objects | Space: Interact with selected object | Z: Cycle through inventory | Shift: Examine inventory item | ESC: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart | ENTER: Start game
