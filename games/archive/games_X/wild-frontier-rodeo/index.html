Wild Frontier Rodeo

Welcome to Wild Frontier Rodeo! You're a fearless rider galloping through the wild frontier, leaping from animal to animal in an endless stampede. Each animal can only carry you for a limited time before bucking you off. Your mission: survive by continuously jumping to fresh mounts, avoiding deadly obstacles, and mastering the art of the rodeo. Complete all 5 levels by staying mounted and alive for the required time. Miss a jump and fall, and it's game over!

SPACE: Jump off current animal to land on another | LEFT ARROW: Tap to steer animal left while riding | RIGHT ARROW: Tap to steer animal right while riding | ENTER: Start game / Continue after level complete | ESC: Pause / Unpause game | R: Restart and return to start screen
