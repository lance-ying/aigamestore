🐵 Monkey Defense 🎯

Monkey Defense is a tower defense game where you strategically place monkey towers to pop balloons before they reach the exit. Each popped balloon gives you money to buy and upgrade more towers. Place your towers wisely, manage your economy, and survive all 15 waves to win the game!

🎮 Arrow Keys: Move cursor | Space: Place tower | Z: Change tower type | Shift: Upgrade tower | Enter: Start wave/game | Esc: Pause | R: Restart
