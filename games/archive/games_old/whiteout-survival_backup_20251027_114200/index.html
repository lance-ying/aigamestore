Whiteout Survival

Survive the frozen wasteland! Move your hero around the map, gather resources, upgrade your city, recruit heroes, and defeat enemies. Complete level objectives to advance through 5 challenging levels and become the strongest!

How to Play:
Move your hero (blue circle) with Arrow Keys or WASD
A green circle shows your interaction range
Walk near resources, combat zones, or your city base
Press SPACE to interact when objects are highlighted
Press Z at your city base to open the upgrade menu
Complete the objectives shown on screen to win each level!
