Mob Multiplier Mayhem

Lead your mob army to victory! Fire projectiles through multiplier gates to spawn units that automatically march toward the enemy base. Deploy powerful champions strategically to break through obstacles and boost your forces. Destroy the enemy base before time runs out to advance through three increasingly challenging levels. Master your aim, timing, and resource management to achieve the highest score!

Arrow Left: Rotate cannon counter-clockwise | Arrow Right: Rotate cannon clockwise | Space: Fire projectile | Shift: Deploy Tank Champion (breaks obstacles) | Z: Deploy Speed Champion (moves faster) | Enter: Start game | ESC: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart to main menu
