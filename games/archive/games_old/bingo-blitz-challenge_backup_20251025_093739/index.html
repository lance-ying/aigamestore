Bingo Blitz Challenge

Welcome to Bingo Blitz Challenge! You have a 5x5 Bingo card with numbers 1-75. Numbers are called one by one - quickly mark them on your card to achieve BINGO (5 in a row: horizontal, vertical, diagonal, or 4 corners). Complete each level by reaching the target score within 3 minutes. Use boosters strategically to maximize your score and progress through 4 increasingly challenging levels!

Arrow Keys: Navigate squares on your Bingo card | Space: Mark the currently selected square / Activate Instant Mark Booster | Shift: Activate Score Multiplier Booster (2x points for 5 seconds) | Z: Activate Free Mark Booster (mark any square regardless of called number) | Enter: Start game / Confirm selections | ESC: Pause / Unpause game | R: Restart and return to start screen
