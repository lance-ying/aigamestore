Terrain Scramble

Navigate your physics-based vehicle across challenging procedurally-generated terrain! Your goal is to travel the required distance for each level without running out of fuel or crashing. Collect fuel canisters to keep moving, perform flips for bonus points, and master the art of balancing speed with control. Complete all three levels to win!

ENTER: Start game from start screen | Arrow Right: Accelerate forward | Arrow Left: Brake / Reverse | ESC: Pause / Unpause game | R: Return to start screen
