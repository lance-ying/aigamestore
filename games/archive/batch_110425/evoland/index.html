EVOLAND

Evoland is a retro adventure where your game world literally evolves as you play! Start in a primitive monochrome realm and unlock new features like color, scrolling camera, and enhanced combat by discovering evolution chests. Battle enemies, collect treasure, and progress through the story to transform your simple adventure into a full-featured RPG experience. Defeat the final boss to win!

Arrow Keys: Move character in 4 directions | Space: Attack enemies / Interact with chests | Z: Activate dodge roll (unlocked later) | Enter: Start game from title screen | Esc: Pause/Unpause game | R: Return to title screen after game over
