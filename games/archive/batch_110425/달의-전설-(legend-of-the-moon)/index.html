달의 전설 (Legend of the Moon)

Navigate through dangerous dungeons filled with monsters in this action RPG. Defeat enemies to collect gold and powerful equipment that enhances your stats. Progress through increasingly difficult rooms, manage your skills and cooldowns, and survive to reach the final boss. Your goal is to clear all rooms in the dungeon and defeat the boss without letting your HP reach zero.

Arrow Keys: Move your character | Space: Basic attack (melee range) | Z: Use Skill 1 - Fireball (ranged attack, 5s cooldown) | Shift: Use Skill 2 - Heal (restore HP, 10s cooldown) | Enter: Start game | ESC: Pause/Unpause | R: Return to start screen
