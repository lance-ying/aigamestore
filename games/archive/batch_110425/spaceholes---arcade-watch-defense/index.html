SPACEHOLES - Arcade Watch Defense

Defend your position in the center of a circular spacehole tunnel against waves of alien bugs! Your spaceship automatically fires at enemies approaching from the outer edges. Survive increasingly difficult waves as enemies spawn faster and new enemy types appear. Rack up points by destroying enemies, but beware - one collision ends your mission. How long can you survive in the depths of space?

Controls: Left Arrow - Rotate counter-clockwise | Right Arrow - Rotate clockwise | ENTER - Start game | ESC - Pause/Unpause | R - Restart
