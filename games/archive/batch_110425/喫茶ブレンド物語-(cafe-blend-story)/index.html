喫茶ブレンド物語 (Cafe Blend Story)

Welcome to Cafe Blend Story! You are the owner of a small cafe with big dreams. Serve customers by creating delicious drinks and food recipes, manage your cafe layout, and earn money to expand your business. Customers arrive with different preferences and moods - satisfy them to earn revenue and reputation. Unlock new ingredients, upgrade your cafe with furniture, and work towards achieving a prestigious five-star rating. Balance customer satisfaction, efficient service, and strategic expansion to build the ultimate cafe empire!

Arrow Keys: Navigate menu cursor / Move selected item | Space: Select/Confirm action (place furniture, serve customer, combine ingredients) | Z: Cancel/Go back | Shift: Open quick menu (recipe creation, furniture placement, view stats) | Enter: Start game | ESC: Pause/Unpause game | R: Restart game (from game over screen)
