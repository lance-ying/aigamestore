서울 2033: 후원자

Navigate post-apocalyptic Seoul in 2033. Survive by making critical decisions that affect your Health, Stress, Money, and abilities. Manage resources wisely, unlock achievements, and discover story branches. Game ends at 0 Health, 100 Stress, or after 30 days of survival.

Controls: ↑↓ Navigate choices | SPACE Select choice | Z Quick-select | ENTER Start | ESC Pause | R Restart
