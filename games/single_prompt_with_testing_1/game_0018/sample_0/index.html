Extreme Landings Pro

Pilot a realistic aircraft through challenging landing scenarios. Navigate using your instruments, manage critical systems like engines and flaps, and respond to emergencies. Complete missions by landing safely within strict parameters while dealing with system failures, weather conditions, and fuel management. Your goal is to touch down smoothly on the runway centerline with proper speed and alignment.

W/S: Pitch control (nose up/down) | A/D: Roll control (bank left/right) | Arrow Left/Right: Rudder (yaw control) | Arrow Up/Down: Throttle (engine power) | Space: Deploy/Retract landing gear | Shift: Toggle flaps (5 settings) | Z: Deploy spoilers | Enter: Start game | ESC: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart from game over
