八荒修仙录 - Eight Desolations Cultivation

Embark on a cultivation journey in the mystical world of the Eight Desolations. You are a cultivator seeking immortality by gathering spiritual energy (Qi), advancing through cultivation stages, and defeating demonic beasts. Collect Qi orbs scattered across the realm, complete cultivation breakthroughs by reaching stage requirements, and battle enemies using your learned techniques. Choose your path wisely—will you achieve Foundation Establishment and beyond, or fall to the dangers that lurk in this Xianxia world?

Arrow Keys: Move your character in four directions | Space: Attack nearby enemies with your cultivation technique | Z: Use special ability (Spirit Burst - area damage, consumes Qi) | Enter: Start the game from the start screen | ESC: Pause/Unpause during gameplay | R: Restart and return to start screen from game over
