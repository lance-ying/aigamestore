WORMS

Command your team of 4 worms in tactical turn-based warfare on a destructible battlefield. Each worm has 100 HP. Aim and fire weapons like Bazookas and Grenades to damage enemy worms or knock them into water hazards. Eliminate all enemy worms to win! The battlefield terrain is destructible - explosions create craters. Watch the wind indicator as it affects projectile trajectories.

Arrow Left/A: Move worm left | Arrow Right/D: Move worm right | Arrow Up/W: Jump | Space: Cycle through weapons (Bazooka, Grenade, Airstrike) | Z: Aim weapon left (decrease angle) | S: Aim weapon right (increase angle) | Shift: Increase power | Arrow Down: Decrease power | Enter: Fire weapon / Start game | ESC: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart (from game over screen)
