盛世天下～女帝への道～ I

Journey through ancient China as a young woman rising to power in "盛世天下～女帝への道～ I" (Path to Empress). Navigate 16 challenging chapters filled with critical choices that shape your destiny. Each decision affects your Charm, Wisdom, and Courage stats while determining survival. One wrong choice leads to immediate game over - choose wisely! Collect rare items, unlock hidden stories, and prove you have what it takes to become Empress. High difficulty, permadeath, and multiple branching paths await.

Arrow Keys (↑/↓): Navigate through dialogue options
Space: Confirm selected choice
Z: View current stats and inventory
Enter: Start game from title screen
ESC: Pause/Unpause game
R: Return to title screen after game over
