Reckless Drift Racing

Race against AI opponents on a muddy isometric track! Master drifting by steering into turns at high speed, manage your vehicle's momentum, and cross the finish line first. Navigate through checkpoints while avoiding track boundaries. Win races to earn points and unlock victory!

Arrow Up/W: Accelerate | Arrow Down/S: Brake/Reverse | Arrow Left/A: Steer Left | Arrow Right/D: Steer Right | Space: Boost (limited use) | Enter: Start Game | ESC: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart to Menu
