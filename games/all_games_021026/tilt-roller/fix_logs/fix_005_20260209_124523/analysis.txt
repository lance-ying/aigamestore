- Issue identified: The ball phases through the platform when stopping or moving slowly. This is caused by two factors: 1) Large `deltaTime` steps (e.g., from frame rate drops) causing the ball to tunnel through the floor in a single frame due to gravity integration. 2) The collision resolution logic for "inside" or "near-surface" states was simplistic (forcing UP), which could be unstable or incorrect if the ball tunneled deep enough or was near a wall/edge.
- Files affected: `physics.js`
- Changes needed: 
    1. Implement a fixed timestep loop (sub-stepping) in `PhysicsSystem.update` to ensure physics runs at a consistent 60Hz (dt=1/60) regardless of render frame rate. This prevents tunneling.
    2. Refactor `resolveSphereBox` to use a robust "closest face" algorithm when the ball is inside or very close to a block, ensuring it is pushed out the shortest path (up, down, or sideways) instead of blindly pushing up.
