- Issue identified: The player physics often fails to detect landings on platforms, causing the player to fall through or slide off. This is caused by two factors: 1) The vertical collision tolerance (0.5) is too small relative to falling speed, causing tunneling. 2) The player's collision box was shrunk uniformly (including Y axis), creating a gap between the player's feet and the platform, leading to jittery ground detection and missed collisions.
- Files affected: `physics.js`
- Changes needed: 
    1. In `handlePlatformCollisions`, modify the bounding box setup to only shrink horizontally (X/Z) for wall sliding, but keep the vertical (Y) bounds accurate to the mesh to ensure reliable ground contact.
    2. In `handleSingleBlockCollision`, increase `landingTolerance` from 0.5 to 0.9 to allow snapping to the platform top even if the player has fallen deep into it within a single frame (ledge assist).
    3. Remove the strict upper bound check for landing to rely on the AABB intersection, preventing rejection of valid landings.
