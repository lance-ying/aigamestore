Skul: The Hero Slayer

Guide Skul, the lone skeleton survivor, through a perilous platforming adventure to rescue the captured Demon King. Battle through waves of Imperial soldiers, collect powerful skull transformations, and defeat the corrupted boss at the end. Master the art of skull-swapping to adapt your combat style and overcome increasingly difficult challenges in this action-packed rogue-lite platformer.

Arrow Keys: Move left/right, jump (up arrow) | Z: Attack with current skull | Space: Switch between equipped skulls | Shift: Special ability (dash/power attack depending on skull) | Enter: Start game | ESC: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart (from game over screen)
