GEM DEFENSE

Defend your realm against endless waves of demonic monsters! Place magical gems on towers to attack enemies along their path. Combine gems to create more powerful versions, and strategically upgrade your defenses. Survive all waves to claim victory!

Controls: 1/2/3: Select gem type • Space: Build/Place gem • Shift: Upgrade • Z: Sell • Enter: Next wave • ESC: Pause • R: Restart
