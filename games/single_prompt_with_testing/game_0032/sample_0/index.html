Stern Continent - Threat of the Dragon

Welcome to Stern Continent! You are the village chief tasked with developing a thriving settlement and protecting it from the menacing dragon. Gather resources, construct buildings, assign villagers to work, craft equipment, and train hunters to form alliances that battle giant creatures. Manage your village economy wisely—process raw materials into refined goods, craft potions, and trade with merchant ships. Your ultimate goal is to build a powerful enough force to defeat the dragon threatening your land.

Controls: Arrow Keys: Navigate/select buildings | Space: Interact with selected building/resource, assign workers | Z: Open crafting menu or send alliance on expedition | Shift: Speed up time (hold to accelerate) | Enter: Start game | ESC: Pause/unpause | R: Restart
