PAL FRONTIER

Explore a dangerous world filled with mysterious creatures called Pals. Capture wild Pals by weakening them in battle, then assign them to work stations to gather resources and build structures. Manage your hunger and health while defending against hostile poachers who want to steal your Pals. Build a thriving base with farms and factories, breed stronger Pals, and survive long enough to establish a prosperous settlement. Win by reaching 1000 prosperity points through successful resource management and base building!

Arrow Keys: Move | Space: Attack | Z: Capture | Shift: Interact/Build | Enter: Start | ESC: Pause | R: Restart
