CUPHEAD: BOSS BATTLE

Battle through intense boss fights as Cuphead! Face off against a challenging boss with multiple attack patterns. Dodge projectiles, dash to avoid danger, and shoot your way to victory. Collect power-ups to enhance your abilities. Defeat the boss to win, but be careful - you only have 3 lives!

Controls: Arrow Left/Right: Move | Arrow Up: Jump | Space: Shoot | Shift: Dash | Z: Parry | Enter: Start | ESC: Pause | R: Restart
