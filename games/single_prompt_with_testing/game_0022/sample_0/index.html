GRIS - Journey Through Sorrow

Guide Gris, a young girl dealing with sorrow, through a fading world that comes alive as she gains new abilities through her magical dress. Collect colored orbs to unlock powers: red for double jump, blue for ground pound, and yellow for dash. Navigate platforms, solve light puzzles, and restore color to the world. There's no death or failure—only exploration and growth. Reach the top of the world to complete your emotional journey.

Arrow Keys (←/→): Move left and right | Arrow Key (↑): Look up to see above | Space: Jump (press again in air for double jump with red ability) | Down Arrow (↓): Ground pound (smash through fragile platforms with blue ability) | Shift: Dash forward (quick movement with yellow ability) | Enter: Start the game | ESC: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart (return to start screen)
