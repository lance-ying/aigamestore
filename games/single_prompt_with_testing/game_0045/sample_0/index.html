NEON STRIKER

You are Neon White, an assassin racing through a neon-lit heaven to exterminate demons. Collect Soul Cards to shoot enemies or sacrifice them for powerful parkour abilities like dashing and double-jumping. Complete the level by eliminating all demons as fast as possible. Master the card system to discover shortcuts and achieve the best time!

Controls: Arrow Keys: Move left/right, aim up/down | Space: Jump (double jump available) | Z: Shoot current card / Use card ability (discard) | Shift: Dash forward (when holding dash card)
