NEON WHITE

You are Neon White, an assassin competing in Heaven's demon-slaying trials. Race through neon-lit obstacle courses, eliminating demons to reach the goal portal. Collect Soul Cards that function as weapons - use them to shoot demons or discard them for powerful parkour abilities. Master the art of card management to find the fastest routes and achieve the best completion times.

Controls: WASD/Arrows: Move | Space: Jump/Use Ability | Shift: Sprint | Z: Shoot | 1-3: Switch Cards | ENTER: Start | ESC: Pause | R: Restart
