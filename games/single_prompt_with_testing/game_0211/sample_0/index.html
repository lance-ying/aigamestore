SKY ACE

Sky Ace is an intense aerial combat game where you pilot an advanced fighter jet through dangerous skies. Your mission: destroy enemy aircraft, evade incoming missiles, and eliminate ground targets while surviving waves of increasingly difficult opponents. Collect power-ups to enhance your weapons and shields. Complete objectives to progress through missions and prove yourself as the ultimate ace pilot. The fate of the skies rests in your hands!

Controls: Arrow Keys: Maneuver aircraft | Space: Fire primary weapons | Z: Launch missiles at locked targets | Shift: Activate afterburner for speed boost | ENTER: Start game | ESC: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart from game over
