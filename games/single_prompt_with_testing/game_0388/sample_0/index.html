RED HOT VENGEANCE

You are a hitman seeking vengeance after being betrayed. Fight through waves of enemies across multiple levels, eliminating all hostile targets while following your code: no killing innocents (women and children). Use your weapons strategically, manage ammunition, and survive increasingly difficult encounters. Complete all levels to achieve your revenge.

Arrow Keys: Move | Space: Shoot | Z: Switch weapons | Shift: Sprint | Enter: Start game | Esc: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart
