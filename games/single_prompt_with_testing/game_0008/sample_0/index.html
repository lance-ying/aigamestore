Type:Rider - Typography Platformer

Navigate two connected rolling dots through intricate typographic worlds! Each level is constructed from letters, symbols, and fonts representing different eras of typography. Reach the end of each level while avoiding hazards and solving physics-based puzzles. Collect asterisks (*) for points and hit checkpoints to save your progress. Fall into gaps or touch spikes, and you'll respawn at your last checkpoint. Complete all levels to master the history of typography!

Controls: Arrow Left/A: Move left | Arrow Right/D: Move right | Space/W: Jump (press again in mid-air for double jump) | Enter: Start game | Esc: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart from GAME_OVER
