Silksong: Ascent

Ascend through a haunted kingdom as Hornet, the Princess Knight. Battle through platforming challenges, defeat enemies with your needle and thread, and climb to the peak of this silk-ruled realm. Collect soul essence from defeated foes to power your abilities. Reach the citadel at the top to win!

Arrow Keys: Move left/right | Space: Jump (hold for higher jump) | Z: Attack with needle | Shift: Dash (when unlocked) | Enter: Start game | Esc: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart to title screen
