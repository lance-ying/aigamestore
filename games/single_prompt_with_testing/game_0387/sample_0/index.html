GLADIATOR: Arena of Champions

You are a gladiator slave fighting for your freedom in the arenas of ancient Rome. Battle through increasingly difficult opponents in epic combat, earning gold and glory. Use your sword and shield to attack, defend, and survive. Defeat all enemies in each arena tier to advance. Reach the Grand Stadium and defeat the Champion to win your freedom! Master the combat system: use momentum for powerful strikes, target weak spots, and time your blocks perfectly.

Arrow Keys: Move your gladiator (Left/Right to move, Up to jump) | Z: Attack with sword (direction matters!) | Space: Block with shield | Shift: Sprint (use momentum for stronger attacks!) | Enter: Start game | ESC: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart to main menu
