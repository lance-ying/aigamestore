The Good Time Garden

Explore "The Good Time Garden" - a pulsating, surreal pink world inhabited by strange organic creatures. Your friend is hungry, and you must collect 15 glowing food orbs scattered throughout this bizarre landscape. Navigate through throbbing membranes, avoid peculiar wandering entities, and experience the unsettling beauty of this hand-drawn animated realm. The world breathes and shifts as you move - embrace the wetness!

Controls: Arrow Keys: Move left/right and jump | Space: Collect food when near | ESC: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart | ENTER: Start game
