INSIDE: Dark Escape

You are a boy escaping from a dark facility filled with danger. Navigate through atmospheric environments, avoid deadly obstacles, solve physics-based puzzles, and evade surveillance. Reach the exit portal at the end of each level to progress. The facility grows more treacherous as you advance - stay alert and use your wits to survive.

Arrow Left/Right: Move left and right | Arrow Up: Jump | Arrow Down: Crouch/Interact with objects | Space: Push/Pull objects | Shift: Sprint (when moving) | Z: Action (activate switches/mechanisms) | Enter: Start game | ESC: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart (return to start screen)
