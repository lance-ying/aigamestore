SNAKE VS BLOCKS

Guide your snake through an endless obstacle course! Your snake is made of numbered balls - collect additional balls to grow longer, and break through numbered bricks by crashing into them. Each brick shows a number indicating how many balls you'll lose when breaking through it. Avoid running out of balls or hitting obstacles that are too strong! The game gets faster and more challenging as you progress. Can you achieve the highest score?

Controls: Left Arrow: Move left | Right Arrow: Move right | Enter: Start | ESC: Pause | R: Restart
