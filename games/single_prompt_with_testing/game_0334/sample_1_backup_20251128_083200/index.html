HYPER LIGHT DRIFTER

You are a Drifter, a wanderer seeking lost technologies in a hauntingly beautiful ruined world. Plagued by a mysterious illness, you must explore dangerous lands, battle fierce enemies, collect ancient artifacts, and discover powerful upgrades. Navigate through treacherous terrain filled with traps and hostile creatures. Your goal is to survive long enough to find the sacred healing crystals that might cure your affliction. The world is unforgiving - death comes swiftly to the unprepared.

Arrow Keys: Move | Space: Dash/dodge | Z: Attack with energy blade | Shift: Toggle map | Enter: Start | ESC: Pause | R: Restart
