STELLAR ODYSSEY

Embark on an interstellar journey as a lone starship pilot navigating through a beautiful but dangerous cosmos. Collect cosmic crystals scattered across space while avoiding asteroid fields and hostile enemy drones. Each crystal brings you closer to unlocking the mysteries of the universe. Your mission is to collect all crystals before your ship's energy depletes or you collide with space hazards. The stars are calling—will you answer?

Controls: Arrow Keys: Navigate | Space: Fire | Shift: Shield | Z: Boost | ENTER: Start | ESC: Pause | R: Restart
