Mount Your Friends

Mount Your Friends is a physics-based competitive climbing game where you must climb a tower of previous climbers (and a goat base) to reach the top before time runs out. Each round, players take turns attempting to climb higher than the last successful climber. The tower gets taller and more unstable as more climbers join. If you fail to reach the top before the timer expires, you're eliminated! Master the art of grabbing, pulling, and flinging yourself upward to create massive towers and outlast your opponents.

Controls: Left Arrow: Move left / Grab left | Right Arrow: Move right / Grab right | Up Arrow: Pull yourself up when grabbing | Down Arrow: Let go / Drop down | Space: Jump / Launch yourself | Shift: Sprint (faster movement) | Z: Quick grab (grab nearest limb) | Enter: Start game | Escape: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart to menu
