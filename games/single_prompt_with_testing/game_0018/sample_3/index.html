Rail Runner

Navigate the rails in this high-speed endless runner! Control a character sprinting across three parallel train tracks, dodging oncoming trains, leaping over obstacles, and sliding under barriers. Collect shimmering coins to boost your score as the pace intensifies. The longer you survive, the faster you run and the more challenging the obstacles become. One collision ends your run—stay alert and keep moving!

Controls: Arrow Left/A: Switch to left lane | Arrow Right/D: Switch to right lane | Arrow Up/W: Jump over obstacles | Arrow Down/S: Slide under barriers | Enter: Start game | ESC: Pause/Resume | R: Restart to main menu
