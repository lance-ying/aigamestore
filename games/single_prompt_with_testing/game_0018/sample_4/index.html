Track Runner 3D

Navigate a character running on three parallel train tracks in this fast-paced endless runner. Dodge oncoming trains, jump over barriers, slide under obstacles, and collect coins to increase your score. The game speeds up progressively, testing your reflexes as the challenge intensifies. Survive as long as you can while collecting the maximum number of coins!

Controls: Arrow Keys/WASD: Move & Jump/Slide | Space: Jump | Enter: Start | Escape: Pause | R: Restart
