STAR FETCHERS

You're a street thug armed with a deadly blade, fighting your way through waves of rival gangsters in the notorious Gray Zone. Slice through enemies with precise sword strikes, collect power-ups to boost your abilities, and survive as long as possible while climbing the criminal ranks. The conspiracy deepens with each wave—how far can you make it before the deep state brings you down?

Controls: Arrow Keys: Move | Space: Dash | Z: Slash Sword | Shift: Slow Motion | ESC: Pause | R: Restart
