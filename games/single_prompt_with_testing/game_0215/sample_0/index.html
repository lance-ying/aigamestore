THE WHITE DOOR

Enter The White Door, a psychological point-and-click adventure where you guide Robert Hill through his stay at a mental health facility. Navigate through a strict daily routine split between the monotone reality of the facility and the vibrant world of Robert's dreams. Complete daily tasks, solve puzzles, and help Robert recover his lost memories by bringing color back to his grayscale world. Each successfully completed routine task restores a fragment of color to the environment, ultimately revealing the path to freedom.

Controls: Arrow Keys: Navigate | Space/Z: Interact | Enter: Start | ESC: Pause | R: Restart
