- Issue identified: The "teleportation" bug is caused by an overly generous vertical collision tolerance in the physics engine, causing the player to snap to the top of a platform even when hitting it from the side or significantly below the top edge. The other requests (speed, floatiness, camera) are gameplay tuning adjustments.
- Files affected: `globals.js`, `game.js`, `physics.js`, `camera.js`.
- Changes needed:
  - `globals.js`: Reduce `GRAVITY` and `STARTING_SPEED`. Adjust `JUMP_FORCE` to maintain jump height with lower gravity (floatier feel).
  - `game.js`: Update `resetGame` to use the `STARTING_SPEED` constant instead of a hardcoded value.
  - `physics.js`: Tighten the vertical intersection threshold (`platformTop - 0.2` -> `platformTop - 0.05`) to prevent snapping when falling.
  - `camera.js`: Increase the Y offset of the camera to provide a higher view angle for better gap visibility.
