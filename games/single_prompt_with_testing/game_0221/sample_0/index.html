THE FORGOTTEN CITY

You are a time traveler exploring the ruins of an ancient Roman city trapped in a mysterious time loop. Your mission is to investigate the city, talk to citizens, gather clues about the curse, and make critical decisions that will determine the fate of everyone. Each loop resets when someone commits a sin, killing everyone instantly. Use your knowledge from previous loops to solve puzzles, avoid triggering the curse, and ultimately break the cycle. Collect all 8 ancient tablets to unlock the truth and save the city.

Arrow Keys: Move character through the city | Space: Interact with citizens/objects, advance dialogue | Z: Reset time loop manually (when unlocked) | Shift: Sprint for faster movement | Enter: Start game | ESC: Pause/unpause | R: Restart from title screen
