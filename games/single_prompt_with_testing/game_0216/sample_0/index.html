NIGHT WATCH - ANOMALY DETECTION

You are a security monitor working the night shift for a mysterious organization. Your task is to watch multiple surveillance camera feeds and identify anomalies - objects that move, change, appear, or disappear between camera views. Report anomalies by selecting the correct room and anomaly type before time runs out. Survive until 6 AM by catching enough anomalies while avoiding false reports.

Controls: Arrow Keys: Navigate | Space: Select/Confirm | Shift: Toggle Report Menu | Z: Quick Switch | Enter: Start | ESC: Pause | R: Restart
