Maiden Spell - Magical Battle Arena

Enter a magical arena where cute maidens battle with spectacular spells! Control your magical girl as she dodges colorful projectiles and fires her own attacks. Survive waves of increasingly challenging patterns, collect power-ups to enhance your abilities, and defeat the boss to win. Each spell pattern is unique and beautiful - dodge everything while unleashing your magical counter-attack!

Controls:
Arrow Keys: Move your magical girl in all directions
Space: Fire your magical projectile attack
Shift: Activate special shield ability (limited duration)
Z: Unleash powerful charged spell (when meter is full)
Enter: Start game from title screen
ESC: Pause/unpause during gameplay
R: Restart game from game over screen
