ULTRAKILL: Blood Arena

You are V1, a blood-fueled machine battling through the depths of Hell. Enemies spawn in waves - destroy them with your arsenal to collect their blood and restore health. Movement is key: dash through the arena, jump to dodge attacks. Chain kills quickly for style points and score multipliers. Survive all waves to achieve victory. Death means game over - keep moving, keep killing, keep bleeding.

W/A/S/D: Move | Space: Jump | Shift: Dash | Z: Shoot | Arrow Keys: Aim | Enter: Start | ESC: Pause | R: Restart
