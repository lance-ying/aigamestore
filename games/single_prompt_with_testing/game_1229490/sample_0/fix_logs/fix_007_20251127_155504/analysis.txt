- Issue identified: The WASD movement vectors are not properly aligned with the camera direction. When rotating the camera right (positive cameraAngleX), the movement vectors rotate in the opposite direction (left), causing inverted/weird controls. This is because the rotation direction of the movement vectors doesn't match the camera's rotation direction.

- Files affected: entities.js

- Changes needed: In the Player.move() method, negate the cameraAngleX when applying rotation to the forward and right movement vectors. This will make the movement rotation direction match the camera rotation direction, so that pressing W moves in the direction the camera is facing.
