OneShot: World Machine Edition

Guide Niko, a child with a glowing lightbulb, through a mysterious dying world to restore the Sun Machine. Navigate through increasingly complex puzzle rooms filled with obstacles, switches, and locked doors. The world is aware of your presence - switches remember their states, and your actions have consequences. Reach the Sun Chamber and place the lightbulb to restore light to this forsaken realm. You only have one shot at saving this world.

Arrow Keys: Move Niko | Space: Interact with switches and doors | Z: Pick up/Put down the lightbulb | Shift: Sprint (moves faster) | Enter: Start game | Esc: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart
