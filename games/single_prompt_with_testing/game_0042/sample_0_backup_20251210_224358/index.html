BROFORCE FOREVER

Battle through a destructible world as an elite action hero in this side-scrolling shooter! Rescue prisoners to unlock new Bros with unique weapons and abilities. Destroy enemies, explode barrels, and blast through terrain to reach the extraction helicopter. Watch out for enemy soldiers, turrets, and explosive hazards. Every level is fully destructible - use it to your advantage!

Arrow Keys: Move left/right, aim up/down | Z: Jump | Space: Fire weapon | Shift: Use special ability | ENTER: Start game | ESC: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart to title screen
