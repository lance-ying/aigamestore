CRUMBLE

Navigate your bouncy blue ball through crumbling platforms to reach the goal! Use your grappling tongue to swing across gaps and climb to safety. Platforms decay over time and fall away, so keep moving! Collect stars for bonus points and master the physics-based movement to complete increasingly challenging levels.

Controls: Arrow Left/Right: Move | Space: Jump | Z: Launch/Hold grappling tongue | ESC: Pause | R: Restart
