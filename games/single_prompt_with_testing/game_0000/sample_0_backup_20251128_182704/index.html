Hollow Depths

Descend into the depths of a forgotten kingdom as a tiny knight exploring vast interconnected caverns. Battle through waves of infected creatures, collect soul energy to unlock powerful abilities, and face off against corrupted bosses. Navigate treacherous platforms, discover hidden paths, and grow stronger as you delve deeper into the mysterious underground realm. Can you reach the heart of the kingdom and survive the darkness below?

Arrow Keys: Move left/right, look up/down (for vertical passages) | Space: Attack with nail (sword) | Shift: Dash (unlocked after collecting dash ability) | Z: Cast spell using collected soul (unlocked after first boss) | Enter: Start game | Esc: Pause/unpause | R: Restart to main menu
