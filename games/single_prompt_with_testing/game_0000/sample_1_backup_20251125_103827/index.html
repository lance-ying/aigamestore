Hollow Depths

Explore the haunting depths of Hallownest in this Hollow Knight-inspired adventure! Navigate dangerous caverns, battle infected creatures, collect Soul essence, and discover the Ancient Relic hidden deep within the ruins. Master your combat skills and platforming abilities to survive the darkness below.

Controls: Arrow Keys: Move | Up: Jump (double jump) | Z: Attack | Down+Z: Slam | Space: Dash | ESC: Pause | R: Restart | ENTER: Start
