Pizza Tower Climb

You are Peppino, a fast and agile pizza chef on a mission to reach the top of the Pizza Tower! Sprint, jump, and dash through a vertical platforming level filled with pizza collectibles and obstacles. Collect as many pizzas as possible while racing against time. Reach the exit at the top to win, but watch out - falling off the bottom means game over!

Arrow Left/Right: Move left and right | Arrow Up: Jump | Space: Dash forward (breaks through obstacles, adds speed boost) | Z: Ground pound (when airborne, destroys blocks below) | Enter: Start game | Esc: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart to main menu
