SPIRITFARER

You are Stella, ferrymaster to the deceased spirits. Navigate your mystical boat across the ethereal seas, collecting spirit passengers and caring for them. Gather resources by fishing and harvesting from floating islands, cook meals to keep spirits happy, and fulfill their final wishes. When a spirit is ready, guide them to the Everdoor to release them into the afterlife. Complete all spirits' journeys to win the game.

Controls: Arrow Keys: Move Stella | Space: Jump/Interact | Z: Cook meals | ENTER: Start | ESC: Pause | R: Restart
