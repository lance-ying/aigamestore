Lost in Dreams

Join a brother and sister duo on a magical adventure through dreamlike puzzles! Navigate through enchanted forests, solve creative riddles, and befriend mystical creatures. Work together to collect magical artifacts, unlock mysterious doors, and find your way home. Each puzzle brings you closer to reality as you explore this whimsical world of childhood imagination.

Controls: Arrow Keys (←→↑↓): Move | Space: Switch Character | Shift: Interact | Z: Use Items | Enter: Start | Escape: Pause | R: Restart
