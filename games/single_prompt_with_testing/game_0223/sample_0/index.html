TATTLETAIL

It's Christmas Eve 1998, and you've snuck downstairs to play with your new Tattletail toy early. But this annoying virtual pet won't stop making noise and demanding attention! Keep Tattletail fed, brushed, and charged while exploring your house in first-person view. But beware - Mama Tattletail is hunting you down. Survive until morning by managing your resources and keeping quiet. The house is dark, your flashlight battery is limited, and Mama is always lurking...

Controls: Arrow Keys: Move and Turn | Space: Care for Tattletail | Z: Toggle Flashlight | X: Shake Flashlight | Shift: Sprint | ESC: Pause | R: Restart
