GOOSE CHAOS

You are a mischievous goose wreaking havoc in a peaceful village! Your goal is to complete pranks on unsuspecting villagers by stealing their items, honking to annoy them, and causing general chaos. Complete all the tasks on your to-do list to win the game. Avoid getting caught by villagers who will chase you if you get too close with their belongings. The village is your playground - explore gardens, shops, and the village green to find opportunities for mayhem!

Controls: Arrow Keys: Move | Space: HONK! | Shift: Sprint | Z: Grab/Drop items | Enter: Start | ESC: Pause | R: Restart
