PORTAL CHAMBERS

Navigate through test chambers using your dual portal device. Fire blue and orange portals on white surfaces to create instant teleportation connections. Use portals to reach platforms, cross gaps, and solve physics-based puzzles. Momentum is preserved through portals - enter fast, exit fast! Complete each chamber by reaching the exit door to advance to the next challenge.

Controls: WASD: Move | Arrow Keys: Camera | Space: Jump | Z/Click: Blue Portal | X/Shift: Orange Portal | ESC: Pause | R: Restart | Enter: Start
