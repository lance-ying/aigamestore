Wobbly World

Welcome to Wobbly World! You're a wobbly character exploring a vibrant island filled with jobs, collectibles, and adventures. Your goal is to earn money by completing delivery jobs, collect special items scattered across the island, and unlock new areas. Navigate through the physics-based world, deliver packages to customers, and gather enough treasures to win! Watch out for obstacles and manage your wobbly momentum as you explore this colorful sandbox world.

Arrow Keys: Move | Space: Jump | Z: Interact/Pick up packages | Shift: Sprint | Enter: Start | ESC: Pause | R: Restart
