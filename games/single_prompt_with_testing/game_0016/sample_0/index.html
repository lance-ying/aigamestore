Don't Starve: Wilderness Survival

You are Wilson, a scientist trapped in a mysterious wilderness. Survive by gathering resources (berries, wood, stone) and crafting tools to hunt rabbits for meat. Manage your hunger meter - if it reaches zero, you die. At night, stay near your campfire or darkness will consume you. Craft better tools to gather resources faster and survive long enough to find the mysterious portal that leads home. Can you survive 5 full day-night cycles and escape?

Controls: Arrow Keys: Move | Space: Gather resources/Hunt rabbits | Z: Craft tools | Shift: Light/fuel campfire | 1: Eat berries | 2: Eat meat | Enter: Start | Esc: Pause | R: Restart
