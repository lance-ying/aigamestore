The Binding of Isaac: Dungeon Escape

Escape the depths of a dangerous dungeon filled with hostile creatures! As Isaac, navigate through procedurally-generated rooms, collect powerful items that transform your abilities, and defeat enemies by shooting tears. Find the exit portal to progress through increasingly challenging floors. Survive long enough to reach safety and escape your mother's wrath. Collect hearts to restore health, discover treasure items for power-ups, and master the art of dodging enemy attacks while fighting back.

Arrow Keys: Move Isaac in four directions | Space: Shoot tears in the direction Isaac is facing | Shift: Use special item ability (when collected) | Z: Drop a bomb (when collected) | Enter: Start game from title screen | ESC: Pause/Unpause during gameplay | R: Return to start screen from game over
