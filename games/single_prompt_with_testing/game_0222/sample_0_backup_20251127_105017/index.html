TURBO OVERKILL: PARADISE PROTOCOL

You are Johnny Turbo, a cybernetic bounty hunter with a chainsaw leg and arm rockets. The city of Paradise has been corrupted by the rogue AI Syn. Slide through enemies with your chainsaw, wall-run across neon buildings, and blast through waves of corrupted cyberpunks. Collect cash from kills to upgrade your arsenal. Clear all enemies to progress through increasingly intense waves. Survive long enough and defeat the final boss to save Paradise!

Arrow Left/Right: Move | Arrow Down + Left/Right: Chainsaw Slide | Space: Jump/Wall-run | Shift: Dash | Z: Fire Arm Rockets | Enter: Start | ESC: Pause | R: Restart
