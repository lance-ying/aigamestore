SteamWorld Quest: Card Combat

SteamWorld Quest: Card Combat is a turn-based card battle game where you control a party of three steampunk heroes fighting against waves of enemies. Build your deck from over 30 unique punch-cards, each with different attack, defense, and special abilities. Play 3 cards per turn to execute powerful combos. Defeat all enemies in each battle to progress through 5 challenging encounters. Level up your heroes, earn gold, and strategically upgrade your deck to overcome increasingly difficult foes. Victory comes from mastering card synergies and tactical planning!

Arrow Keys: Navigate menu selections and card choices | Space: Confirm card selection / Execute turn | Shift: View detailed card information | Z: Cancel last card selection | Enter: Start game from title screen | ESC: Pause/Unpause game | R: Return to start screen from game over
