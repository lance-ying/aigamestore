SALLY FACE: Strange Neighbors

You are Sal, a boy with a prosthetic face investigating the dark mysteries of Addison Apartments. Navigate through eerie hallways, collect clues, solve puzzles, and uncover the sinister truth. Use your Gear Boy device to interact with supernatural elements. Watch out for lurking shadows and ghostly apparitions. Collect all evidence pieces to unlock the final mystery and escape the nightmare.

Controls: Arrow Keys: Move | Z: Interact | Space: Gear Boy | Shift: Sprint | ENTER: Start | ESC: Pause | R: Restart
