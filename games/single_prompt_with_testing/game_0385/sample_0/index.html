DRAPLINE

DRAPLINE is a roguelite dragon-raising adventure where you nurture a dragon girl by feeding her various items to build her strength over a year-long journey. Your goal is to train her to defeat the final catastrophe by strategically choosing food items that boost her stats. Feed her meat, fish, gems, and rare items to increase her power, health, and abilities. Each item provides different stat bonuses and unlocks new skills. Battle enemies in turn-based combat using your acquired skills, and reach the final boss to save the world!

Arrow Keys (↑↓): Navigate menus | Space: Confirm selection / Use skill | Z: Cancel / Back | Enter: Start game | ESC: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart
