JAZZPUNK OFFICE INFILTRATION

You're a secret agent in an absurd Cold War world filled with corporate espionage and bizarre gadgets. Explore a surreal office building, collect peculiar objects, interact with strange NPCs, and complete ridiculous missions. Your objective: gather enough intelligence items and find the secret exit to complete your mission. Watch out for security systems and roaming guards!

Arrow Keys: Move agent around the office | Space: Interact with objects/NPCs | Z: Use equipped gadget | Shift: Sprint (moves faster) | Enter: Start game | Esc: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart to main menu
