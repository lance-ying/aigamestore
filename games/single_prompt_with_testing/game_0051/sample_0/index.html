🤖 ROBOT ARENA 🤖

You are a robot gladiator in a deadly arena. Survive waves of enemy robots by slicing them apart with your laser sword. Any part of your body can be severed - lose too many parts and you're destroyed! Dodge enemy attacks, cut them down piece by piece, and survive as long as possible. Collect power-ups dropped by defeated enemies to enhance your abilities.

Arrow Keys: Move | Space: Swing Sword | Shift: Dash | Z: Block | ENTER: Start | ESC: Pause | R: Restart
