Undefined Fantastic Object

Navigate through a mystical bullet-hell shooter as a shrine maiden! Destroy enemies, collect power-ups and mysterious Bentler items to summon UFOs. Chain UFOs for massive points while dodging intricate bullet patterns. Reach the end of each stage to face powerful bosses. Can you uncover the mystery of the flying ship?

Controls: Arrow Keys: Move | Z: Shoot | Shift: Focus (slow move) | Space: Spell Card (bomb) | Enter: Start | ESC: Pause | R: Restart
