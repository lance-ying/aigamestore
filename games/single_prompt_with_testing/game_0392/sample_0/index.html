未確認幻想物体 - Undefined Fantastic Object

Navigate a mystical world as a shrine maiden battling mysterious flying objects in this retro-style bullet hell shooter! Defeat enemies, collect power-ups and special Venturer items to summon helpful UFOs. Master the art of dodging intricate bullet patterns while managing your resources. Reach the top of the screen to auto-collect items, or use slow movement to pull items toward you. Can you survive the onslaught and uncover the mystery of the flying ship?

Arrow Keys: Move | Z: Shoot | Shift: Slow Mode | Space: Spell Card | Enter: Start | ESC: Pause | R: Restart
