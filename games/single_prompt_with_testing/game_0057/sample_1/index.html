Harvest Haven

Welcome to Harvest Haven! You've inherited a small farm that needs restoration. Plant and harvest crops, manage your energy throughout the day, and build up your farming skills. Each day cycle lasts 60 seconds - use your time wisely! Plant seeds, water crops, harvest when ready, and sell your produce to earn gold. Upgrade your skills and unlock new crops as you progress. Watch your energy meter and return home before nightfall to rest for the next day!

Arrow Keys: Move your character around the farm | Z: Plant seeds (costs gold and energy, requires tilled soil) | X: Water crops (costs energy, makes crops grow faster) | C: Harvest mature crops (grants gold and experience) | Space: Till soil to prepare for planting (costs energy) | Shift: Open/close shop to buy seeds | Enter: Start game from title screen | ESC: Pause/unpause game | R: Restart and return to title screen
