🌾 HARVEST HAVEN 🌾

Welcome to Harvest Haven! You've inherited a small farm that needs restoration. Plant and harvest crops through the seasons, manage your energy wisely, and upgrade your tools to become a master farmer. Watch your farm grow as you unlock new seeds and abilities. Can you create a thriving farm and reach level 10 in farming?

Controls: Arrow Keys: Move | Space: Use tool | Shift: Shop | Z: Switch tools | Enter: Start | ESC: Pause | R: Restart
