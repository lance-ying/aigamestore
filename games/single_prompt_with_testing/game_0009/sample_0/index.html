Traffic Run

Navigate your vehicle through dangerous intersections filled with fast-moving traffic. Hold SPACE to accelerate forward and release to brake. Time your movements carefully to slip through gaps in the cross-traffic without colliding. Each successful crossing earns you coins and advances you to more challenging intersections with faster traffic and more lanes. One collision ends your run!

Controls:
SPACE: Hold to accelerate forward, release to brake
ENTER: Start the game from the title screen
ESC: Pause/unpause during gameplay
R: Restart after game over
