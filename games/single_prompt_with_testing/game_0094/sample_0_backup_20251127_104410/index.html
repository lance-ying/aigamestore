SHOVEL KNIGHT: TREASURE QUEST

Embark on a pixel-perfect platforming adventure as the brave Shovel Knight! Dig through mystical ruins, collect glittering treasure gems, and defeat monstrous enemies with your trusty Shovel Blade. Navigate treacherous platforms, discover hidden riches, and prove your worth as the wielder of Shovelry!

Controls:
Arrow Left: Move left | Arrow Right: Move right | Arrow Down: Dig downward while in air (Shovel Drop attack)
Space: Jump (double jump available) | Z: Attack with Shovel Blade
ESC: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart | ENTER: Start game
