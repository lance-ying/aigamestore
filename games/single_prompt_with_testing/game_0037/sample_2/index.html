SONIC THE HEDGEHOG 2

Race through vibrant zones as Sonic the Hedgehog! Collect golden rings to protect yourself from enemies, run at blazing speeds through loops and springs, and defeat robotic badniks. Your goal is to reach the end of each act while collecting as many rings as possible. Collect 50+ rings to access Special Stages where you can earn Chaos Emeralds. Gather all 7 emeralds to unlock Super Sonic's incredible power! Watch out for spikes, pits, and Dr. Eggman's mechanical minions.

Controls: Arrow Left: Move left | Arrow Right: Move right / Build speed | Arrow Down: Crouch / Spin Dash (hold and release) | Space / Arrow Up: Jump / Double Jump (spin attack in air) | Z: Special ability (when Super Sonic is unlocked) | Enter: Start game | ESC: Pause / Unpause | R: Restart (from game over screen)
