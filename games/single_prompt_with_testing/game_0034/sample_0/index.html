Subnautica: Below Zero

Explore the frozen depths of Planet 4546B in this underwater survival adventure. You are a diver searching for your sister in an arctic alien ocean. Navigate through diverse biomes, avoid hostile creatures, collect vital resources, and manage your oxygen and temperature. Build habitats to create safe zones and craft essential equipment. Discover alien artifacts to unlock the mystery. Survive long enough to uncover the truth about what happened here.

Arrow Keys: Move diver (UP/DOWN/LEFT/RIGHT) | Space: Interact/Collect resources and artifacts | Shift: Sprint (uses more oxygen) | Z: Build habitat at current location (costs resources)
