ROAD RUNNER

Escape the relentless police pursuit in this high-speed endless runner! Draw the road ahead of your speeding vehicle to navigate through an ever-scrolling landscape. Avoid crashes, outsmart the cops, and survive as long as possible. The chase intensifies as you go—speed increases, more police cars join the pursuit, and obstacles appear. Rack up points for every second you survive and compete for the highest score!

Controls:
LEFT ARROW: Steer road left | RIGHT ARROW: Steer road right | SPACE: Draw road segment (hold to draw continuously)
ENTER: Start game | ESC: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart
