Lep's World - Jump n Run

Navigate through challenging platformer stages as a brave leprechaun! Jump across gaps, avoid enemies, and collect coins and gold to maximize your score. Manage your health carefully and look for cloverleaf pickups to extend your lives. Complete stages quickly and without taking damage to earn higher ranks (S, A, B, C). Unlock new worlds by achieving A-rank or better on at least 10 stages in each world. Master precise jump timing and smart routing to become a platforming champion!

Arrow Keys / A,D: Move Left/Right | Space: Jump (hold for higher jump) | Shift: Sprint (run faster) | Enter: Start Game / Continue | Esc: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart (return to start screen)
