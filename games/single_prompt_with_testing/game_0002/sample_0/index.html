Traffic Run

Navigate your vehicle through busy intersections by timing your acceleration perfectly. Your car automatically stops at each crossing where traffic flows perpendicular to your path. Press SPACE to accelerate forward through gaps in the traffic. Collect coins for points and avoid collisions to survive. Each successful crossing increases the difficulty with faster and denser traffic. How many intersections can you conquer?

Controls:
SPACE: Accelerate vehicle forward through intersection
ENTER: Start game from start screen
ESC: Pause/unpause game
R: Restart game (from game over screen)
