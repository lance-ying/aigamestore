SHOTGUN STANDOFF

Welcome to Shotgun Standoff - a deadly game of Russian Roulette in an underground arena. Face off against The Dealer in a high-stakes battle where both of you take turns with a loaded shotgun. The shotgun contains a mix of live rounds and blanks. Use items strategically to gain information and survive. Win two rounds to claim victory, but lose your health and it's game over. Only one walks away.

Arrow Keys: Navigate menu | Space: Confirm selection | Z: Quick shoot self | Shift: Quick shoot dealer | Enter: Start game | ESC: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart
