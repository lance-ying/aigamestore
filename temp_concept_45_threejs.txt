Neon White is a single-player speedrunning FPS where you can sacrifice your guns for godlike parkour moves.

Neon White is a lightning fast first-person action game about exterminating demons in Heaven. You are White, an assassin handpicked from Hell to compete with other demon slayers for a chance to live permanently in Heaven.
