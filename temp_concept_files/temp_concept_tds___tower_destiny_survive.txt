► KING OF THE CASTLE

Well technically it’s a tower, but still. It’s yours, as long as you can keep the zombies away from it in this tower defense strategy game. It appears the zombie apocalypse is upon us based on how many of them are roaming the streets, so keep your gun nearby as you work to build your tower and keep it moving despite the waves of zombies.

► CONSTRUCT AND DEFEND

To build your tower, you’ll need to collect different blocks as you travel, which you can then assemble to form your fortress. Within each of the levels you can add different weapons that will help keep you safe and on the go despite the masses of zombies that are trying to get in to eat your brains. Be careful, once your defenses are breached, you’re as good as dead so keep building and moving!

●  Ready, aim, fire – It’s a zombie tsunami! This RPG shooter will keep you busy blasting zombies as you work to build and protect your mobile castle. Wage war on the undead to avoid becoming one with them. Collect new and exciting weapons along the way, and make sure you upgrade them so that your shooter skills remain top notch.

●  Strategize – Use strategy to plow through your enemies as you roam in search of blocks to add to your tower. While there may be a lot of idle game play and zombie shooting, you will need to plan ahead and think about how and where you want to add weapons and more to your fortress. You’ll need all the guns and protection you can find as you advance through the levels and encounter more and more enemies!

●  Level up – Progress through levels in this tower defense RPG, and gain access to more exciting upgrades, weapons, tower parts, and more! The Earth is your oyster if you can remain a survivor and avoid becoming zombified, so fight for your life and keep exploring.

●  So lively – The graphics, not the zombies. Dive into this world of vibrant and cute characters and delightful animations that really bring everything to life. Who knew fighting off zombie waves could be so adorable? Even as you work to come up with the winning strategy you’ll be able to relax and have some fun thanks to the chill vibes.
 
► LAND OF THE LIVING DEAD

So come on then, survivor, build your tower and wage war on the zombies in this fun and engaging strategy shooter game! Use your tower to travel across the lands as you explore and take out enemies, and have fun customizing all the different levels with neat guns and other weapons that will help you in your fight against the undead.
