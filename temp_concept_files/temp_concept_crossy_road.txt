Crossy Road is an endless arcade hopper where players control a character by tapping the screen to hop forward one square or swiping left/right to move one square sideways, navigating an infinitely scrolling, procedurally generated environment. The game features various obstacles like multi-lane roads with fast-moving vehicles, train tracks with trains, and rivers requiring jumps across logs or lily pads, with any collision resulting in instant game over. Players collect scattered coins during runs, which are then used to unlock over 300 unique characters, each potentially introducing new visual themes or worlds. Progression is driven by achieving higher distances to set new high scores and completing daily challenges for rewards, with a local same-device multiplayer mode allowing two players to compete simultaneously.
