**ソニック・ザ・ヘッジホッグ 2** Sonic The Hedgehog 2 is a fast-paced 2D side-scrolling platformer where players control Sonic using an on-screen virtual D-pad for horizontal movement and a jump button to leap, with a second tap activating a spin dash. Levels are filled with loops, springs, and enemies, which are defeated by jumping on them or using a spin attack, while collecting 100 rings grants an extra life and rings protect from single hits. Progression involves completing multiple zones, each with 2-3 acts culminating in a boss battle against Dr. Eggman, with difficulty scaling through increasingly complex level layouts and enemy patterns. Collecting 50 rings and entering a giant ring grants access to a 3D half-pipe Special Stage to collect 7 Chaos Emeralds, which unlock the Super Sonic transformation. Players lose a life if they take damage without rings or fall into a pit, and the game ends upon losing all lives or defeating the final boss.
