Swipe to roll. Stay on the path. Can you make it to the goal?

Swipe your finger to roll the ball through tricky 3D levels.
Guide the ball from platform to platform without falling off!
It’s all about timing, precision, and control.

Features:
 - Simple swipe controls
 - Satisfying physics-based gameplay
 - Hundreds of fun and challenging levels
 - Clean, minimalistic 3D visuals
 - Easy to pick up, hard to master

Stay focused and don’t let the ball fall. One swipe can make all the difference!
