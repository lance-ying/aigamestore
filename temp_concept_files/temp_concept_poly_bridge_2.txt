**Poly Bridge 2:** Poly Bridge 2 is a physics-based puzzle game where players construct bridges to guide vehicles across gaps, using a drag-and-drop interface to place structural elements like roads, wood, steel, and the new Spring material between fixed anchor points. Players select materials from a palette and draw segments by tapping and dragging between nodes, forming a network of supports and roadways, with each material having specific cost and stress resistance properties. After construction, players initiate a simulation phase by tapping a "Start" button, observing how vehicles (e.g., cars, trucks) attempt to cross the bridge, with structural integrity determined by a custom physics engine that calculates stress on each segment. A level is successfully completed when all designated vehicles safely reach their destination without the bridge collapsing, adhering to a strict budget limit (e.g., $10,000) and a maximum number of structural elements. Progression involves completing over 100 pre-designed levels, each introducing new environmental challenges, vehicle types, and mechanics like hydraulic pistons or the bouncy Spring material, with difficulty scaling through increasingly complex terrain and tighter budget constraints.
