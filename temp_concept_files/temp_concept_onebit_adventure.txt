Survive endless dungeons in this turn-based roguelike RPG!
OneBit Adventure is a pixel dungeon crawler where every step is a strategy. Battle monsters, collect loot, and level up unique classes to see how far you can go.

Choose your hero — Warrior, Wizard, Necromancer, Archer, and more — each with unique abilities and playstyles. Explore caves, castles, and the underworld in a world that never ends. Every run is different, every fight a test of survival.

Why You’ll Love It:

Classic turn-based roguelike RPG gameplay

Multiple character classes with unique skills and builds

Endless dungeon crawling with loot and gear upgrades

One-handed controls — easy to play anywhere

Hardcore permadeath mode for roguelike veterans

Global leaderboards and competitive rewards

Play offline or online anytime

Retro 8-bit pixel art with modern polish

If you enjoy roguelike adventures like Shattered Pixel Dungeon or survival RPGs like Vampire Survivors, you’ll feel right at home.
