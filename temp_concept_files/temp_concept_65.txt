**EXIT – Die Prüfung des Greifen**: This single-player digital escape room game immerses players in a haunted castle, where they interact with static, hand-drawn scene illustrations by tapping on hotspots to examine objects, collect items, and uncover clues. Players manage an inventory of collected items, combining them by dragging one item onto another or using them on specific environmental elements to solve one of the twenty distinct, multi-layered puzzles. Progression is linear, with each successful puzzle solution unlocking new areas or revealing critical codes, and difficulty scales as puzzles require more complex multi-step deductions and item interactions. The core loop involves observing scenes, collecting information, and applying logical reasoning to solve each unique challenge, with the ultimate goal being to escape the castle by completing all twenty interconnected puzzles.
