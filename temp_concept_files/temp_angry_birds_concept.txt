**앵그리버드 2 (Angry Birds 2)**: Angry Birds 2 is a physics-based puzzle game where players use a slingshot to launch various birds at structures housing green pigs, aiming to destroy them and retrieve stolen eggs. Players control the slingshot by dragging back to adjust trajectory and power, then releasing to fling a chosen bird, with some birds allowing a tap mid-flight to activate a special ability. Levels are multi-stage, requiring strategic bird selection from a limited roster to dismantle complex pig fortifications and defeat boss pigs. Progression involves completing hundreds of levels, earning feathers to level up individual birds and increase their scoring power, and collecting cosmetic hats. Players can also compete in the Arena against others for leaderboard rankings or join Clans to collaborate on challenges.
