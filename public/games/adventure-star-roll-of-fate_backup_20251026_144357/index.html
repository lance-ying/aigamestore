Adventure Star: Roll of Fate

Navigate through 4 progressively challenging grid-based levels to reach the final exit and win! Move tile by tile, interact with mysterious events that can help or harm you, and manage your HP carefully. Each interactive tile triggers a random event—treasure, traps, enemies, or surprises. Your luck influences outcomes, but skill in navigation and risk management is key. Reach the exit of each level to advance, but beware: if your HP drops to 0, it's game over!

Arrow Keys or WASD: Move your character (Up/Down/Left/Right)
Space: Interact with event tiles (when standing on them)
Enter: Start the game from the main menu
Esc: Pause/Unpause the game
R: Restart (return to main menu from game over screen)
