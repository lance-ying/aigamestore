Sushi Sprint

Welcome to Sushi Sprint! Manage your sushi restaurant by serving customers efficiently. Take orders, prepare dishes at kitchen stations, serve food, and collect payments before customers lose patience. Earn gold to unlock recipes and upgrade your kitchen. Complete level objectives within the time limit to progress through 5 increasingly challenging levels. Keep your reputation high and don't let too many customers leave unhappy!

Arrow Keys: Scroll camera view | Space: Pause/Unpause game | R: Restart to main menu | Enter: Start game from menu | ESC: Pause game
