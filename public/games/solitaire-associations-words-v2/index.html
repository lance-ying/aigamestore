Solitaire Associations: Words

Match word cards to their category cards by strategically placing them on the board. Start with a partially filled board and draw cards one at a time from the deck. Place each word card onto its matching category card to build complete stacks. Clear all cards within the move limit to win the level. Run out of moves before completing all stacks and you lose. Plan carefully – each level challenges you with more complex associations and tighter move restrictions!

Arrow Keys (Left/Right): Navigate between available cards and stacks | Arrow Keys (Up/Down): Select category stack destinations | Space: Draw a card from the deck / Place selected card | Z: Undo last move (if available) | Enter: Start game from title screen | Esc: Pause/Unpause game | R: Return to start screen
