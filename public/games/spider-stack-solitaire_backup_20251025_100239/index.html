Spider Stack Solitaire

Spider Stack Solitaire is a challenging card game where you must clear a tableau of 104 cards by building 8 complete King-to-Ace sequences of the same suit. Move cards between columns to form descending sequences, flip hidden cards, and strategically use your 5 deals to complete all sequences. Progress through 4 levels of increasing difficulty from 1 suit to 4 suits. Plan your moves carefully to maximize your score and minimize the number of moves!

Arrow Keys: Navigate between cards when a card is selected | Space: Auto-move selected card to first valid column, or auto-hint if no card selected | Z: Undo last move | ENTER: Start game from start screen | ESC: Pause/Unpause game | R: Restart (return to start screen)
