Gridlock Duel

Challenge an AI opponent in this strategic grid-based battle! Place your X marks strategically to form a line before the AI completes its O line. Progress through 5 increasingly difficult levels with larger grids and smarter AI opponents. Win rounds to advance, earn points, and set a high score. One mistake means starting over from Level 1!

TAP-BASED CONTROLS: Arrow Keys: Tap to move cursor one cell (release and tap again to move) | Space or Z: Tap to place your X mark | Enter: Start game | Esc: Pause/unpause | R: Restart
