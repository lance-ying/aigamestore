Throw Master 2D

You are a skilled throwing agent tasked with eliminating hostile enemies while protecting innocent hostages. Aim carefully and throw knives to take down enemies before they reach the hostages. Use exploding barrels to damage multiple enemies at once, and break wooden boxes to stun nearby foes. Complete all 5 levels by eliminating every enemy while keeping all hostages safe. Strategic thinking and precise aim are key to success!

Arrow Left/Right: TAP to rotate aiming direction | Space: Throw knife in aimed direction | Enter: Start game from menu | Esc: Pause/Unpause game | R: Restart and return to start screen
