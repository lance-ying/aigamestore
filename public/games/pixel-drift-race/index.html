Pixel Drift Race

Race through increasingly challenging tracks in this top-down arcade racer! Navigate your car through traffic, avoid obstacles, and master the art of drifting. Progress through suburban streets, busy city roads, and treacherous mountain passes before facing off against the ultimate boss car. Collect cash to upgrade your vehicle's engine, acceleration, and handling. Complete each level without losing all your health to advance. Can you defeat The Enforcer and claim victory?

TAP-BASED CONTROLS:
Arrow Up / W: TAP to Boost Speed (~45 mph increase per tap)
Arrow Left / A: TAP to Move Left One Lane
Arrow Right / D: TAP to Move Right One Lane
Space: TAP to Brake (reduces speed)
Enter: Start Game
Esc: Pause / Unpause
R: Restart (return to start screen)

Note: All controls are tap-based. No keys need to be held down. Each tap triggers one action!
