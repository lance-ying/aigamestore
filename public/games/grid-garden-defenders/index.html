Grid Garden Defenders

Grid Garden Defenders is a lane-based tower defense game where you must protect your garden from waves of zombies. Plant Sunflowers to generate sun currency, deploy Peashooters to attack zombies, and use Wall-nuts as defensive barriers. Zombies march down 5 lanes trying to reach your house. If any zombie reaches the left edge, you lose! Complete all 5 increasingly challenging levels to win. Manage your sun resources wisely and place plants strategically to defend against Basic and Conehead Zombies.

ENTER: Start the game from the main menu | 1, 2, 3: Select plant type (Sunflower, Peashooter, Wall-nut) | Arrow Keys: Move plant placement cursor on the grid | SPACE: Place selected plant at cursor position | Z: Activate Plant Food on the plant at cursor position (if available) | ESC: Pause/Unpause the game | R: Restart and return to main menu
