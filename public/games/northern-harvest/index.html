Northern Harvest

Welcome to Northern Harvest! Build and manage your frontier farm in the wild north. Plant crops, raise animals, construct buildings, and complete quests from Kate and Paul to grow your homestead. Explore expedition sites to gather rare resources. Progress through 5 farm levels by earning XP and coins. Your goal: reach Farm Level 5, complete all main quests, and fully upgrade your farmhouse to achieve prosperity!

Arrow Keys: Pan the farm view | Space: Quick harvest/collect all ready items on screen | Shift + Click: Multi-select plots for batch actions | Z: Plant selected crop (after selecting from seed menu) | Enter: Start game from menu | Esc: Pause/unpause game | R: Restart (return to start screen)
