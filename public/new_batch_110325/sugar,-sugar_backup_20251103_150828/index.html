Sugar, Sugar

Guide falling sugar particles into cups by drawing barrier lines! Each level requires you to fill all cups to their target amounts. Sugar falls from spawners and bounces off your drawn lines. Watch out for special elements: color filters change sugar colors, gravity switches flip the flow direction, and teleporters instantly transport particles. Plan your barriers carefully to direct the right amount of sugar into each cup. Complete all requirements to advance through increasingly challenging puzzles!

SPACE: Activate cursor and toggle drawing mode
ARROW KEYS: Move cursor (when active) or draw barrier lines (when in drawing mode)
D: Delete the most recently drawn barrier
R: Restart current level (when in GAME_OVER states)
ENTER: Start game from start screen
ESC: Pause/Unpause game
