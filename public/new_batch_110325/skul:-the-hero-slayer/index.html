Skul: The Hero Slayer

Control Skul, a brave skeleton warrior, through dangerous dungeon rooms filled with enemies. Collect unique skulls that transform your abilities, gather powerful items to enhance your stats, and earn Dark Quartz to unlock permanent upgrades. Each run is a fresh challenge - defeat enemies, swap skulls strategically, and survive as long as possible. Death means losing your current items and skulls, but your Dark Quartz remains for permanent progression!

Arrow Keys: Move left/right, navigate menus | Up Arrow: Jump | Space: Attack with current skull's ability | Z: Swap between collected skulls | Shift: Dash (quick evasive movement) | Enter: Start game from title screen | Esc: Pause/Unpause game | R: Return to start screen after game over
