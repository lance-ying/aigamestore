Solo Tú Por Aquí

Navigate through a surreal dreamscape filled with mysterious puzzles and hidden secrets. Explore interconnected zones, interact with strange objects, and uncover fragments of a cryptic narrative. Your goal is to find keys, solve environmental puzzles, and unlock the small door that leads to escape. Each choice you make shapes your path toward one of multiple endings. Can you discover the main secret hidden within this twisted reality?

Arrow Keys: Move forward/backward and turn left/right
Space: Interact with objects in front of you
Z: Toggle between walking and running speed
Enter: Start the game from the title screen
ESC: Pause/unpause the game
R: Return to the start screen
