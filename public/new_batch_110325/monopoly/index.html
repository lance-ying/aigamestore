MONOPOLY

Experience the classic property trading game in digital form! Roll dice to move around the board, buy properties, build houses and hotels, and collect rent from opponents. Strategic property management and smart investments are key to bankrupting your opponents. Watch out for Chance and Community Chest cards that can dramatically change your fortune. The last player standing wins!

Arrow Keys: Navigate menu selections and UI choices | Space: Roll dice / Confirm actions | Z: Buy property / Build house / End turn | Shift: Auction / Decline property | Enter: Start game | ESC: Pause/Unpause game | R: Restart to main menu
