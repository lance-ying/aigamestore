The House of Da Vinci

Explore a mysterious Renaissance workshop filled with intricate mechanical puzzles inspired by Leonardo Da Vinci's inventions. Each room contains interconnected puzzle elements—gears, levers, and hidden compartments—that you must manipulate to unlock the next area. Use the special Oculus Perpetua device to reveal hidden mechanisms and solve the mystery of the master's disappearance. Navigate through multiple rooms, solving increasingly complex puzzles to reach the final revelation.

Arrow Keys: Look around the workshop (rotate camera view) | Z: Interact with puzzle elements in front of you (activate/manipulate) | Space: Toggle Oculus Perpetua vision (reveals hidden mechanisms) | Enter: Start game from title screen | ESC: Pause/Unpause game | R: Restart game (return to title screen)
