MEGA RUNNER - Robot Master Challenge

Battle through 9 challenging levels with progressive difficulty! Face Robot Masters that grow stronger with each stage. Early levels ease you in with simpler platforming and weaker bosses, while later stages test your skills with complex patterns and fierce enemies. Master weapon switching, learn boss weaknesses, and survive deadly hazards to reach the ultimate victory!

Arrow Left/Right: Move left and right | Arrow Up: Aim weapon upward | Arrow Down: Aim weapon downward (when airborne) | Z: Jump | Space: Shoot (hold direction + Space to aim) | Shift: Cycle through unlocked weapons | ENTER: Start game | ESC: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart (return to title screen)
