Court Clash: P5 Tennis

Welcome to Court Clash: P5 Tennis! Face off against increasingly skilled AI opponents in intense 1-on-1 tennis matches. Your character automatically moves to intercept the ball - focus on perfecting your shots! Win rallies by making your opponent miss, hit out of bounds, or fail to clear the net. First to score 3 points wins the match. Complete all 4 levels to become the Tournament Champion!

ENTER: Start the game from the main menu | ARROW KEYS: Control shot direction and power during gameplay | SPACE: Execute your shot / Pause and resume the game | ESC: Pause and resume the game | R: Restart and return to the main menu | Z: Quick return to main menu from any screen
