Gem Duel Tactics

Gem Duel Tactics is a turn-based Match-3 puzzle game where you compete against an AI opponent to score the highest points. Match 3 or more gems of the same type by swapping adjacent gems. Create cascading combos for bonus points! Charge your special Booster ability by matching blue star gems, then unleash it for strategic advantages. Complete 4 increasingly challenging levels by outscoring the AI within the turn limit. Master the board, plan your moves carefully, and become the ultimate Gem Master!

Arrow Keys: Move cursor on the grid | Space: Select/swap gems, confirm menu options | Shift: Activate your charged Booster ability | Enter: Start game from title screen | Escape: Pause/unpause game | R: Restart and return to title screen
