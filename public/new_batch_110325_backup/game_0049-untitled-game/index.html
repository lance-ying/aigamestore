Duck Life 7: Battle

Train your duck and become the ultimate champion! Build up your duck's stats through training mini-games, then test your skills in turn-based battles against rival ducks. Collect rewards to unlock powerful equipment that boosts your abilities. Progress through increasingly difficult opponents to prove you're the world's greatest duck battler. Victory comes from strategic training and smart combat decisions!

Controls: Arrow Keys: Navigate menus and select actions | Space: Confirm selection and perform actions | Z: Cancel/Back and open Equipment menu | ENTER: Start game | ESC: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart
