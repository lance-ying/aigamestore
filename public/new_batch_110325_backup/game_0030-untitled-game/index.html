WINGSPAN

WINGSPAN is a strategic bird collection game where you build habitats and attract birds to your sanctuary. Play bird cards by spending food tokens, activate habitat rows to trigger bird powers, and collect eggs to score points. Complete 4 rounds of strategic turns to build the best bird sanctuary and achieve the highest score. Birds provide unique abilities and chain reactions when you activate their habitat row!

Arrow Keys (↑/↓/←/→): Navigate menus and select options | Space: Confirm selection / Take action | Z: Cancel / Go back | Enter: Start game from menu | ESC: Pause/Unpause game | R: Restart (return to start screen)
