Boxville 2: The Mystery Adventure

Guide a red can character through a hand-drawn city to solve a mystery. Explore interconnected scenes, collect items, and solve visual puzzles to progress. Use your inventory items on objects in the environment to unlock new areas. Complete all puzzles across multiple locations to reach the final scene and win the game. No text—everything is told through animated visuals and thought bubbles.

Arrow Keys: Move character left/right | Space: Interact with highlighted objects | Z: Open/close inventory | Shift: Use selected inventory item | ESC: Pause/unpause | R: Return to start screen | ENTER: Start game
