森丘露营地 - Forest Hill Campsite

Welcome to Forest Hill Campsite! Manage your pixel-art camping ground by placing facilities to attract campers. Build fishing spots, campfire BBQ areas, and tent pitches across different biomes. Satisfy campers' wishes to earn currency, unlock new facilities, and expand your campsite. Your goal is to achieve a 5-star rating and attract 100 campers by strategically managing your layout and resources.

Arrow Keys: Navigate menu/select facilities | Space: Place selected facility/confirm | Z: Cancel/Remove facility | Shift: Toggle shop mode | ESC: Pause/Unpause | ENTER: Start game | R: Restart to start screen
