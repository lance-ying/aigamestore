SteamWorld Quest

SteamWorld Quest is a turn-based card battle game where you command a party of heroes against enemy robots. Build your strategy by playing up to 3 cards per turn from your hand, spending Steam energy to unleash attacks, defenses, and special abilities. Defeat all enemies to win battles, earn gold and experience to level up your heroes, and unlock powerful new cards. Victory comes from smart deck building and tactical card play!

Arrow Keys (←→): Navigate cards | Space: Confirm/Play cards | Z: Cancel selection | Shift (hold): View deck stats | Enter: Start game | Esc: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart
