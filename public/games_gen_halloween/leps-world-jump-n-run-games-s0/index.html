Lep's Adventure

Guide Lep the leprechaun through a challenging platformer adventure! Navigate through levels filled with enemies, collect gold coins for points, and grab cloverleaves to restore health. Jump on enemies to defeat them, avoid falling into pits, and reach the flag at the end of each level to progress. You have 3 health points - lose them all or fall into a pit and you'll restart the level. Can you master all the worlds and collect every coin?

Controls: Arrow Keys (←/→): Move left and right | Space: Jump (hold for higher jump) | Enter: Start the game from the start screen | Escape: Pause/unpause during gameplay | R: Restart from game over screen
