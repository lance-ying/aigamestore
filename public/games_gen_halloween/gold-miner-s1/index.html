Gold Miner

Control a gold miner's mechanical claw to extract valuable items from underground mines! Your claw swings automatically left and right - press SPACE to deploy it downward to grab gold nuggets, diamonds, and rocks. Each item has different values: small gold ($50), large gold ($500), diamonds ($600), and rocks ($10). Heavier items slow down the claw's retraction speed. Meet the money target within the time limit to advance to the next level with higher goals and more complex layouts.

SPACE: Deploy claw downward to grab items
ENTER: Start the game
ESC: Pause/unpause the game
R: Restart to main menu (from game over screen)
Arrow Keys: Navigate shop menu (between levels)
