Long Neck Run

Navigate an endless runner as a character with a growing neck! Collect rings matching your current color to extend your neck, avoid wrong-colored rings that shrink it, and duck under or jump over obstacles. Your neck must be long enough to clear barriers and ziplines. The track gets faster and more complex over time. Survive as long as possible and achieve the maximum distance!

Controls: Arrow Left: Move left | Arrow Right: Move right | Arrow Up: Jump | Arrow Down: Duck | ENTER: Start game | ESC: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart
