Cut the Rope: Time Travel

Guide the candy to feed both Om Nom and his ancestor by cutting ropes and activating physics-based mechanisms. Each level features suspended candy that must reach both monsters to complete the stage. Collect all three stars along the way for a perfect score. Navigate through historical eras with unique challenges - bubbles that float candy upward, air cushions that bounce objects, and gravity zones that alter physics. Strategic timing and rope cutting are essential to success!

Arrow Keys (←/→): Select which rope to cut or which device to activate | Space: Cut the selected rope or activate the selected device | R: Restart level (during gameplay) | Enter: Start game / Continue to next level | Esc: Pause / Unpause game
