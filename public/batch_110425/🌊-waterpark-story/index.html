🌊 Waterpark Story

Welcome to Waterpark Story! Build and manage your dream water park by placing pools, slides, and facilities on a grid. Attract guests who generate income and satisfaction points. Serve them food and drinks, gift them items like tubes and swimsuits, and they'll become your SNS friends, helping your park grow. Expand your park, unlock new attractions, and create a highly-rated destination that keeps guests happy and coming back for more!

Arrow Keys: Navigate menu and select grid tiles
Space: Place selected item or interact with guests
Z: Open/close build menu or cancel action
Shift: Speed up guest movement (hold)
Enter: Start game
Esc: Pause/unpause game
R: Restart (return to start screen)
