Five Nights at Freddy's 3

Survive five nights in a haunted horror attraction office from 12 AM to 6 AM each night. Track the deadly animatronic Springtrap through security cameras and use audio lures to keep him away from your office. Seal vents to block his entry routes. Phantom animatronics will cause system failures that must be rebooted quickly. If Springtrap reaches your office, it's game over. Each night gets progressively harder with faster system failures and more aggressive behavior.

Controls: Arrow Keys: Navigate tablet | Space: Activate system | Z: Toggle tablet | Enter: Start | ESC: Pause | R: Restart
