The House of Da Vinci III

Navigate through Leonardo da Vinci's mysterious workshop by solving intricate mechanical puzzles across three interconnected rooms. Use the legendary Oculus Perpetua to view and manipulate the past, altering objects to affect the present timeline. Collect items, examine objects closely, and combine tools to unlock mechanisms. Each puzzle solved opens the next chamber, leading you deeper into da Vinci's secrets. Complete all three rooms to escape and uncover the final revelation.

Arrow Keys: Rotate camera view | Space: Interact with object | Z: Toggle Oculus Perpetua | Shift: Examine object | 1-3: Select inventory item | Enter: Start game | Esc: Pause | R: Restart
