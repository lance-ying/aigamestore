Shadow Knight - Ninja Fighting Action RPG

You are a Shadow Knight, a legendary ninja warrior fighting through waves of dark fantasy creatures. Battle through multiple missions, defeating enemies to earn gold and equipment. Upgrade your attack and health stats between missions, unlock powerful skills, and progress through increasingly challenging encounters. Your goal is to complete all missions and defeat the final boss. If your health reaches zero, you must retry the mission.

Arrow Left/Right: Move left and right | Arrow Up: Jump | Space: Basic attack (melee slash) | Z: Special skill - Shadow Strike (dash attack with cooldown) | Shift: Ultimate skill - Ninja Fury (area damage with longer cooldown) | Enter: Start game from start screen | Esc: Pause/unpause game | R: Return to start screen from game over
