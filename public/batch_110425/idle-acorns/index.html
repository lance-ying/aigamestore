Idle Acorns

Idle Acorns is a progressive resource management game where you build a thriving woodland economy. Start by collecting acorns manually, then purchase upgrades to automate your production. Unlock four distinct areas: the Shop for upgrades, the Fishing Pond for catching fish, the Garden for growing crops, and the Campfire for cooking and trading. Craft 24 unique items, upgrade passive income sources, and watch your acorn empire grow. There's no game over—just continuous expansion and achievement!

Controls: Arrow Keys: Navigate between areas | Space: Collect acorns/Cast line/Plant seeds/Cook | Z: Purchase/Catch fish/Harvest/Trade | Shift: Toggle automation | Enter: Start game | Escape: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart
