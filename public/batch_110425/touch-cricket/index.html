TOUCH CRICKET

Touch Cricket is a fast-paced batting simulation where you face increasingly challenging bowling deliveries. Time your shots perfectly by combining footwork and directional strokes to score runs. Each ball requires precise timing - mistimed shots can lead to getting out. Build your score while managing your 10 wickets across different delivery types including fast balls, spinners, and yorkers. Survive and score big to progress through career levels!

Controls: W/↑: Front Foot | S/↓: Back Foot | A/←: Off Side | D/→: On Side | SPACE: Defense | SHIFT: Power Shot | Z: Late Cut | ENTER: Start | ESC: Pause | R: Restart
