OCTAGON RUSH

Navigate through an infinite octagonal tunnel that scrolls toward you at increasing speeds. Your avatar stays fixed in the center while the tunnel rotates around you. Align yourself with clear paths to avoid obstacles like speed bumps. Survive for 60 seconds to win, or push for the highest score before time runs out or you crash. The tunnel accelerates and obstacles become more complex as you progress!

Controls: Left Arrow: Rotate tunnel clockwise | Right Arrow: Rotate tunnel counter-clockwise | Space: Flip 180 degrees | Enter: Start game | Esc: Pause/unpause | R: Restart
