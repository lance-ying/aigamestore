Card Conqueror

Card Conqueror is a turn-based deck-building roguelike where you battle through a series of enemies using cards. Each turn you draw cards that cost energy to play, allowing you to attack enemies or defend yourself. After each battle, you can add new cards to your deck, improving your strategy as you progress. Your goal is to defeat all enemies and reach the final boss to win the game.

UP/DOWN: Navigate menu options and select cards in your hand
LEFT/RIGHT: Switch between viewing your draw pile and discard pile during battle
SPACE: Play the currently selected card during your turn
Z: End your turn when you're done playing cards
SHIFT: View enemy intentions during battle
ENTER: Start the game and confirm selections
ESC: Pause the game
R: Restart the game
