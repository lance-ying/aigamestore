Battle Zone

Battle Zone is a top-down tactical shooter where you control an elite soldier navigating a dangerous battlefield. Eliminate enemy soldiers while avoiding their fire and collecting ammo. Complete the mission by eliminating all enemies or reaching the extraction point. Watch your health and ammunition - tactical positioning and smart resource management are key to victory!

Arrow Keys: HOLD to move your soldier smoothly
Z: TAP to fire weapon
SPACE: TAP to dash forward
SHIFT: TAP to toggle cover/crouch mode
ENTER: Start game from title screen
ESC: Pause/unpause game
R: Restart game after victory or defeat
1-4: Switch weapons
