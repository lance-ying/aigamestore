Connect Cascade

Connect Cascade is a color-matching puzzle game where you connect adjacent dots of the same color to clear them from the grid. Form squares to clear all dots of that color! Complete level objectives like clearing specific colored dots or dropping anchors to the bottom before running out of moves. Progress through 5 increasingly challenging levels that test your strategic thinking and pattern recognition.

TAP-BASED CONTROLS: Arrow Keys: Single tap moves cursor one cell | Space: Tap once to start path, move with arrows to build, tap again to execute | Shift: Cancel current path | Enter: Start game | Escape: Pause/unpause | R: Return to main menu
