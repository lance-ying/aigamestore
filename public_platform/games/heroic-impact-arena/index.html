Heroic Impact Arena

Command a team of 3 Heroes in tactical turn-based combat against waves of Villains across 5 challenging levels. Select your Heroes, choose their abilities wisely, and target enemies strategically. Master the elemental system where Fire beats Nature, Nature beats Water, and Water beats Fire. Manage ability cooldowns and status effects to emerge victorious. Defeat all enemies to progress through levels and achieve the highest score!

TAP-BASED Controls (VLM Compatible): All controls use single key taps, no holding required. Arrow Left/Right: Select Hero or Target | Arrow Up/Down: Select Ability | Space: Confirm | Z: Cancel | Shift: End Turn | Enter: Start | Escape: Pause | R: Restart
