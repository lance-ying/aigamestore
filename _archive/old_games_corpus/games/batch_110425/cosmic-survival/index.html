Cosmic Survival

Survive waves of Cthulhu monsters in this intense top-down survival shooter. Eliminate enemies to gain experience and level up, choosing from powerful skill upgrades to enhance your abilities. Survive for 5 minutes to win, or see how long you can last. Your health depletes when hit - avoid enemies and use your growing arsenal of abilities to stay alive!

Arrow Keys: Move your character | Space: Fire your weapon | Z: Activate special ability (when unlocked) | Enter: Start the game | ESC: Pause/Unpause | R: Return to start screen from game over
