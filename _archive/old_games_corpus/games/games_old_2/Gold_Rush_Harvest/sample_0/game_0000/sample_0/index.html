Gold Rush Harvest

Welcome to Gold Rush Harvest! Build and manage your northern farm through 5 challenging levels. Plant crops, raise livestock, produce goods, and fulfill orders to earn gold and experience. Unlock new territories and embark on expedition mini-games to gather rare resources. Complete all story objectives to achieve the ultimate Klondike Dream and win the game!

ENTER: Start game • ARROW KEYS: Navigate grid • SPACE: Interact with objects • SHIFT (hold): View details • Z: Open/Cancel menus • ESC: Pause • R: Restart
