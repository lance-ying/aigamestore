Penguin Dash: Arctic Escape

Guide your penguin through the treacherous Arctic in this fast-paced lane runner! Switch between three lanes, jump over obstacles, slide under barriers, and collect fish while rescuing fellow penguins. Complete all three levels to escape the frozen wilderness. Watch out for octopuses and gaps in the ice! Use power-ups wisely to boost your survival chances.

Arrow Up: Jump over low obstacles and gaps | Arrow Down: Slide under high obstacles | Arrow Left: Move to left lane | Arrow Right: Move to right lane | ENTER: Start game from menu | ESC: Pause/Unpause game | R: Restart (return to menu)
