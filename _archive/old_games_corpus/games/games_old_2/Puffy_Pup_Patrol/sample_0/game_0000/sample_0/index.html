Puffy Pup Patrol

Welcome to Puffy Pup Patrol! Your beloved dog is feeling stressed and its head is swelling up! Your mission is to calm your pup by tracing soothing comfort patterns that appear over its head. Watch the Swelling Meter carefully - if it reaches 100%, your pup becomes too distressed! Complete all 5 levels by successfully healing your dog with accurate and speedy pattern traces. Each level introduces new challenges including faster swelling, more complex patterns, and pesky distractions. Can you become the Master Healer?

ENTER: Start the game from the start screen | ARROW KEYS: Draw comfort patterns (move cursor to trace the target shape) | SPACE: Confirm drawing / Advance screens | ESC: Pause / Unpause the game | R: Restart and return to start screen | SHIFT: Alternative pause key
