ノベルズローグ ～異世界古書堂と封印の魔女～

Enter the mystical Ancient Bookstore and venture into one of four sealed books, each a roguelike dungeon with unique card-based combat. Build your deck strategically by selecting cards and relics after each battle. Fight through floors of increasing difficulty, defeat powerful bosses, and break the seals to unlock new chapters. Your run ends when your health reaches zero or you triumph over the final boss!

Arrow Keys: Navigate menu selections and target enemies in combat | Space: Confirm selection, play selected card in combat | Z: Cancel/Go back in menus, End turn in combat | Shift: View detailed card information (hold) | Enter: Start game from title screen | ESC: Pause/Unpause during gameplay | R: Return to title screen from game over
