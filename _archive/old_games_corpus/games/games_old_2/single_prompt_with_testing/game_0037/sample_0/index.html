SONIC RUSH

Race through colorful zones as Sonic the Hedgehog! Collect golden rings for protection and points, defeat enemies by jumping on them, and navigate through loops and platforms. Reach the goal post at the end of each act to progress. Lose all your rings when hit by an enemy, and lose a life if you're hit without rings. Collect 100 rings for an extra life. Complete all acts and defeat Dr. Eggman to win!

Arrow Left/A: Move left | Arrow Right/D: Move right | Space/W: Jump (press again in air for spin dash attack) | Enter: Start game | ESC: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart (from game over screen)
