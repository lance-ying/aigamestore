Alien Shooter: Survive

Battle through endless waves of alien monsters in this intense top-down survival shooter! Collect experience points to level up and unlock powerful abilities and upgrades. Survive as long as possible while enemies grow stronger and more numerous every 20 seconds. Your health is precious - avoid taking damage and use strategy to outlast the alien hordes!

Arrow Keys: Move your soldier in all directions | Space: Fire your weapon | Z: Use active ability (unlocked at level 2) | Shift: Toggle auto-aim on/off | Enter: Start game from start screen | Esc: Pause/unpause during gameplay | R: Return to start screen from game over
