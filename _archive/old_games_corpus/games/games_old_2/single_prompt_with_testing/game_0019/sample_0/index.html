SpongeBob: The Cosmic Shake

Guide SpongeBob through cosmic platforming challenges across mystical wish worlds! Navigate treacherous platforms, defeat enemies, and collect cosmic coins. Unlock special abilities like Karate Kick and Hook-Swing to access new areas and overcome obstacles. Complete objectives, find checkpoints, and reach the portal to advance. Manage your 3 hearts carefully - fall into hazards or lose all health, and you'll respawn at the last checkpoint!

Controls:
Arrow Keys / WASD: Move SpongeBob left and right
Space: Jump (double jump when unlocked)
Z: Karate Kick attack / Break barriers
Shift: Hook-Swing (when near swing points and unlocked)
Enter: Start game from menu
Escape: Pause/Unpause game
R: Restart from game over screen
