Navigate through a haunting dreamscape filled with memories and nightmares. You are the only one here, or are you? Explore eerie environments, interact with mysterious objects, and solve environmental puzzles to uncover the truth. Find clues scattered throughout the dream world to piece together what happened and discover the path to escape. Your choices and discoveries will determine which of multiple endings you'll reach. The deeper you go, the more unsettling things become.

Arrow Keys: Move forward/backward and turn left/right | Space: Interact with nearby objects | Shift: Sprint | Z: Toggle flashlight | Enter: Start game | Esc: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart
