WINGSPAN

Build your nature preserve by strategically placing birds across three habitats (Forest, Grassland, Wetland) to score the most victory points. Play birds by paying food and egg costs, activate habitat powers to gain resources, and create powerful chain reactions. The game lasts 4 rounds with decreasing actions (8→7→6→5). Score points from bird values, eggs laid, and end-of-round goals. Highest score wins!

Arrow Keys: Navigate menu options and select habitats/actions | Space: Confirm selection, play card, or execute action | Z: Cancel current selection or go back | ENTER: Start game from title screen | ESC: Pause/unpause game | R: Return to start screen from game over
