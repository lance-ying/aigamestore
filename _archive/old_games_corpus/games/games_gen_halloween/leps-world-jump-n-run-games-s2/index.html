Lep's Adventure

Guide Lep the Leprechaun through a colorful platformer adventure! Navigate through treacherous terrain, leap over chasms, and dodge dangerous enemies like angry bees and crawling beetles. Collect shimmering gold coins to boost your score and grab cloverleaf power-ups to restore your health. You start with 3 hearts - don't let them run out! Reach the flag at the end of each level to progress. Can you master all the challenges and achieve the highest score?

Arrow Left: Move left | Arrow Right: Move right | Space: Jump (hold for higher jumps) | Enter: Start game from title screen | ESC: Pause/Unpause game | R: Restart (return to title screen)
