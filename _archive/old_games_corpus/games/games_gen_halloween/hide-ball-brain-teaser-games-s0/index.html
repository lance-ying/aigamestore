Hide Ball: Brain Teaser Game

Hide Ball is a physics-based puzzle game where you must protect good balls (green) from monster balls (red). Use your limited moves to strategically place blocks and barriers in the environment. After you've used all your moves, the monster balls activate and seek out the good balls. Keep all good balls safe for 5 seconds to win the level!

Arrow Keys / WASD: Move selected block | Space: Select/Deselect block | Enter: Start game | Esc: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart to menu
