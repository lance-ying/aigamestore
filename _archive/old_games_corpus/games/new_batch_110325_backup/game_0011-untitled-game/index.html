KNIFFEL Classic

Roll the dice and achieve the highest score in this strategic dice game! You have 13 rounds to fill your scorecard with different combinations like Three of a Kind, Full House, or the coveted Kniffel (five of a kind). Each turn, roll up to three times, strategically holding dice between rolls. Choose wisely—each category can only be used once! Compete against AI opponents and aim for the top score.

Controls: ENTER: Start game | SPACE: Roll dice / Continue | Arrow Keys (LEFT/RIGHT): Navigate categories | Arrow Keys (UP/DOWN): Select dice | Z: Confirm category | ESC: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart
