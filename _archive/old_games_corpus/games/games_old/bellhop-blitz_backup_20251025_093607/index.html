Bellhop Blitz

Welcome to Bellhop Blitz! You're managing a bustling hotel where efficiency is key. Guide your bellhop to clean dirty rooms, check in waiting guests at reception, and collect payments from departing guests. Each level presents a larger hotel with more rooms and faster-paced challenges. Hire staff to automate tasks and upgrade your abilities to handle the rush. Meet revenue targets and purchase essential upgrades before time runs out to advance through 4 increasingly complex hotels. Keep guests happy – if too many leave unsatisfied, it's game over!

Controls: Arrow Keys / WASD: Move bellhop | Space: Interact with nearby objects | Shift: Open/Close upgrade menu | Enter: Start game | Esc: Pause/Unpause | R: Restart
