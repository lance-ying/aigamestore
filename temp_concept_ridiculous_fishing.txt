**Ridiculous Fishing - A Tale of Redemption** Players initiate a fishing attempt by tapping to cast, then tilt their device left and right to steer the lure, avoiding all fish as it descends to reach maximum depth, which can extend to over 500 units. Once the lure hits a fish or reaches its maximum depth, it automatically begins ascending, and players must rapidly tap the screen to fire their equipped weapon, shooting as many fish as possible that appear on screen. Each caught fish converts into cash based on its rarity and size (e.g., a common fish yields 5 coins, a rare one 50), which players use in a shop UI to purchase upgrades like longer fishing lines (increasing max depth by 50 units), faster lures (increasing descent speed by 15%), and more powerful weapons (increasing projectile count by 1 or damage by 20%). Reaching specific cumulative cash milestones (e.g., 1000, 5000, 10000 coins) or catching unique fish species unlocks new fishing locations (continents), each featuring distinct fish populations, increased depth limits, and more challenging fish patterns during descent and ascent. The primary objective is to complete Billy's narrative journey by progressing through all fishing spots, catching all 60+ unique fish species, and fully upgrading all available equipment to maximize catch efficiency and income.
